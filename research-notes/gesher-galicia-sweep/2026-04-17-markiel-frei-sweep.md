# Gesher Galicia AGD Search Sweep — 2026-04-17

**Method.** 7 searches on `app.geshergalicia.org/agd`, plus follow-up narrowing queries. Phonetic toggle varied per search (noted). Query params used: `phonetic={true|false}&surname=X&given_name=Y&page_number=N`. The `town` parameter is not URL-routable; I relied on surname/given-name filtering and on locality chips in the results.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Date of Marriage | 21 AUG 1894 |
| Groom | Hersch MARKEL, Waniowice |
| Groom's Father | **Mordko Ber MARKIEL**, Waniowice |
| Groom's Mother | **Henie MARKEL**, Waniowice |
| Bride | **Lea FREI**, age 29 (b. c. 1864–1865), Waniowice |
| Bride's Father | **Jozef FREI**, Waniowice |
| Bride's Mother | **Taube FREI**, Waniowice |

**Citation:** Sambor Jewish Marriages (1891-1897). Central Archives of Historical Records in Warsaw (AGAD), Fond 424, File 103, Record 34, Scan 52, Page 52. Identifier #137:295.

**Implications.**
1. Every principal in the record is from **Waniowice**, not just the couple. Confirms the family's pre-marriage residence and gives Jozef/Taube FREI as an independently anchored Waniowice couple.
2. **Hersch's father is Mordko Ber Markiel, not Nissen Mendel.** A Tier 1 primary source (civil marriage register, created 1894) names the father as Mordko Ber. The "Nissen Mendel" attribution (tertiary tradition) is now contradicted and should be reclassified as speculative, with the 1894 marriage record as the decisive record.
3. Lea's stated age of 29 gives her a birth year of c. 1864–1865 (Sambor/Waniowice area).
4. Hersch's mother is recorded as **Henie MARKEL** — her maiden name is recorded as MARKEL, same as the groom's surname. This is either (a) endogamous marriage within the extended Markel family (common in Galicia), (b) her maiden name was simply omitted and the register defaulted to her married surname, or (c) a transcription shortcut. Flag for further investigation.

## Method notes for future sessions

- The AGD iframe is cross-origin from the parent geshergalicia.org page. Browser automation works best against the iframe URL directly: `https://app.geshergalicia.org/agd/?phonetic={true|false}&surname=X&given_name=Y&page_number=N`.
- The `town`/`locality` URL parameter is silently stripped. Locality filtering requires the UI button ("Any" → picker), which was not needed here because Markiel/Frei surnames are distinctive enough to filter by inspection.
- Each result card has an expand button that reveals the full structured detail (Date, Name, Town, Parents, Age) plus a "Show details" sub-button that reveals repository/fond/file/scan/identifier. Both are JS-driven (no href).
- Phonetic ON broadens Markel ↔ Markiel ↔ Merkel ↔ Mortko ↔ Mordche — helpful when chasing variant spellings but adds many false positives.
