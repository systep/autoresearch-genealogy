# Deych / Rokiskis Sweep — 2026-04-17

## Headline outcome

**No direct record for Zawel Deykh, Rochel Tsine Deych, or any Deych/Deich/Daych/Deutsch household in Rokiskis surfaced via public sources.** All targeted Rokiskis surname lists and Yizkor indices were searched; the Deych surname is absent.

But this is **not** a dead end:
- The relevant records are indexed and held at LVIA Fond 1226 (Kovno gubernia crown rabbinate metrical records).
- The JewishGen Lithuania Vital Records Database covers Rokiskis births/marriages/deaths 1874-1896 — Rochel Tsine's birth (~1861) is too early, but the presumed 1877 marriage to Levi Itzhak Zalmanson IS in range and should be in the index.
- Access requires authenticated JewishGen / LitvakSIG search (login-walled) or an LVIA records request.

## Rokiskis name-list surveys — negative results

Surveyed without finding Deych / Deich / Daych / Deutsch or Zalmanson / Salmanson:

- **Rokiskis 1908 Family List** (JewishGen KehilaLinks): complete, no hits.
- **Rokiskis 1897 Census extract** (60+ surnames listed in the public extract): no hits. Caveat: the 1897 Kovno gubernia census survives only in fragments (~5.5% of records).
- **Rokiskis Necrology** (29 surnames in JewishGen KehilaLinks): no hits.
- **Rokiskis Yizkor Book** (pages 370-373 and 452-524 sampled): no Deych, no Zalmanson. One article heading reads "Zalman the Soda-Water Maker" — a first name only.
- **FamilySearch catalog** query `place:Rokiskis + subject:Jewish`: 0 results.
- **Yad Vashem Central DB**: not directly searchable from agent environment (SSL cert issue); user should search manually at https://collections.yadvashem.org/en/names.

Meaning: absence from these fragmentary/post-1877 lists does not refute Zawel's presence in Rokiskis c. 1876-1878. The 1877 marriage record, if located at LVIA, is still the decisive document.

## Tangential Zalmanson lead (Tier 3, needs corroboration)

**Rabbi Levi Yitzchok Zalmanson (1812-1872)** — Geni profile 6000000002764523421 — son-in-law of the Tzemach Tzedek (Menachem Mendel Schneerson, 3rd Lubavitcher Rebbe), co-head of the Lubavitch yeshiva from 1837.

Children on Geni:
- Rabbi Shneur Zalman Zalmanson
- Mordekhai Ber Zalmanson
- Yosef Chaim Zalmanson
- Rivkah Schneerson
- Pearl Schneerson

**Significance.** The Rokiskis Yizkor Book describes Rokiskis as "a fortress of the Lubavitch branch of the Hasidic movement in Lithuania." **Levi Itzhak Zalmanson** (husband of Rochel Tsine Deych, father of Barnett/Berko) carries exactly the naming pattern of a descendant or namesake of this rabbi. The dates are also consistent: the Rokiskis Levi Itzhak (who had a son Berko b. 1883) could plausibly be a grandson or grand-nephew of the 1812-1872 Rabbi Levi Yitzchok.

This overlaps with vault Open Question R6 (Zalmanson-Schneerson connection), which is already marked **Resolved — Strong Signal** tracing Srul Mendel and Rabbi Levi Yitzchok Zalmanson as brothers, both sons of the Shneuri lineage (Rivka Beila, daughter of the Mitteler Rebbe). If the Rokiskis connection holds, it specifically places the Levi Itzhak (grandfather-generation) branch in Rokiskis, which would explain why Berko/Barnett's mother Rochel Tsine (a Rokiskis native) married into this line.

**Follow-up for the user**: cross-check this Geni profile against the existing vault research on Rivka Beila Shneuri and Srul Mendel Zalmanson — the Rokiskis locus may tighten an already-resolved question.
