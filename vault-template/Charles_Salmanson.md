## Biography

Charles Salmanson was born September 1, 1914, the third son of Barnett (Berko) Salmanson and Elizabeth (Salk) Salmanson. He was preceded by brothers Leonard Irving (1910) and Samuel (1912), and followed by siblings Martha (~1919) and Donald (1924).

Charles was part of the family that founded **Adams Drug Company** in 1932. Along with his father Barnett and brothers Leonard, Samuel, and Donald, he helped grow the company from its origins in Woonsocket and Pawtucket, Rhode Island to a chain of over 500 stores in 14 states.

He married **Hannah Kushner** (1914-2001), daughter of Jacob and Dora (Alpert) Kushner of Providence, Rhode Island. Hannah's siblings included Esta (Kushner) Katz of Providence, Samuel Kushner, and Phillip Kushner. Their children were **Jerrold A. Salmanson** (b. 1942, Providence; d. 2024) and **Deborah Salmanson** (later Roberts).

Jerrold A. Salmanson worked alongside his cousin Alan Carl Salmanson in the family business. Samuel Salmanson's 2004 obituary referred to "his nephews, Jerrold and Alan," confirming the cousin relationship (sons of two different brothers: Jerrold from Charles, Alan from Leonard).

Hannah (Kushner) Salmanson predeceased Charles, dying in 2001. She is memorialized on Find a Grave #185239892, which lists "Jerrold A. Salmanson of Providence" as a surviving child.

Charles died July 7, 2008.
