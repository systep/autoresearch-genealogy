## Biography

Willy Friedberg is known only from the 24 November 1910 New York marriage certificate of his daughter [[Rebecca_Marcus_Friedberg]], which records his name and Russian birthplace. Given Rebecca's Libau (Liepāja, Latvia) birthplace circa 1879, Willy was likely a resident of Libau or the Kurland/Courland governorate in the 1870s-1880s, born himself in the mid-19th century.

The witness Leo Friedberg at Rebecca's 1910 wedding is almost certainly Willy's son (Rebecca's brother), but this is not explicitly documented.

"Willy" is likely an Americanization or informal form of a Germanic/Yiddish given name such as Velvel (Wolf) or Wilhelm.
