## Biography

Jozef Frei (Yichus: Yoseph / Yusef) was a resident of Waniowice in the Sambor district of Galicia. He is confirmed at Tier 1 as the father of **[[Leah_Frei]]** by the 21 August 1894 Sambor Jewish Marriages register (AGAD Fond 424 File 103 Record 34), which records him as "Jozef FREI" of Waniowice. His wife Taube (= "Toba Rivka" in the Yichus) was also listed as of Waniowice on that record. This confirms both parents' residence in Waniowice at the time of Lea's marriage.

Per the Yichus narrative (Ruth Gold, 2011, Tier 2), he was the father of four daughters: Leah, Bashe, Feiga, and Frieda Frei Sternback (the first of the family to emigrate, settled Brooklyn NY).

**Note on alternate AGD records.** The Gesher Galicia All Galicia Database contains two other Jozef Frei records (Jozef Samuel FREI death 1900, Pruchnik; Jozef Szmul FREI birth 1902, Lutowiska). Both are held at the Przemyśl State Archive, in towns geographically distant from Sambor (80-100 km away). Research on 2026-04-17 concluded that **these records are NOT the same Jozef Frei**; they belong to unrelated Frei families. Our Jozef Frei has no surviving independent record in AGD beyond his appearance as father-of-bride on the 1894 register.

Confidence: **Moderate**. Tier 1 evidence for fatherhood of Lea and residence in Waniowice. No independent record of his own birth, death, or other biographical details.
