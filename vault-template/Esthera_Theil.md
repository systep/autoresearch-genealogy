## Biography

Esthera Theil was born on 9 October 1791 (per GEDCOM). Per the **MUTTER church certificate** (Tier 1, 4 April 1938, Pastor A. Bogeschdorfer), she was person (D): "Esthera geb. Petrus Theil aus Maimburg" (Esthera, born of Petrus Theil, from Malmkrog/Maimburg). The "Petrus" may be a patronymic indicating her father's given name was Petrus.

She married **(C) [[Johann_Krause_Binder]]** (eigentlich/actually Krauss) on **15 January 1817**. Their son was [[Martin_Binder]] (b. 3 May 1831).

**Birthplace correction:** The GEDCOM gives Waldhütten as her birthplace, but the MUTTER certificate (Tier 1) specifies "aus Maimburg" (from Malmkrog, Romanian: Malancrav), a separate Transylvanian Saxon village. Tier 1 supersedes.
