# Hypothesis: Parents of Johann Michael Brecht II (1706-1794)

## Hypothesis Statement

The father of emigrant [[Johann_Michael_Brecht]] (b. 28 May 1706 Schriesheim, d. 13 Sep 1794 Reading, PA) was **Johannes Michael Brecht** (b. 12 Oct 1662 Schriesheim; d. 11 Aug 1719 Schriesheim), and his mother [[Anna_Catherina_Bright_Brecht]] was **Anna Katarina Hoffman** (b. 14 Oct 1664; d. after 1726), daughter of Hans Jost Hoffman and Christina Frank. They married 29 Jul 1684 in Schriesheim.

Note: the vault file `Anna_Catherina_Bright_Brecht.md` was previously populated from a MyHeritage GEDCOM with a 1734 PA birth date, which is almost certainly a distinct later descendant (great-granddaughter?), NOT the 1726 emigrant widow. The 1726 emigrant widow is Anna Katarina Hoffman Brecht, born 1664 in Schriesheim.

## Status: SUPPORTED (Moderate Signal)

Multiple independent tertiary/secondary sources converge; primary register inspection on archion.de (Schriesheim Evangelical) would upgrade to Strong.

## Evidence For

### 1. Father: Johannes Michael Brecht (1662-1719)
- Familypedia, WikiTree Brecht-58, and Geni all independently identify the 1726 emigrant widow's husband as **Johannes Michael Brecht**, b. 12 Oct 1662 Schriesheim, d. 11 Aug 1719.
- Son of **Hans Balthasar Brecht (1636-1703)** (already in vault as [[Balthasar_Hans_Brecht]]) and **Anna Margaretha Christman** (b. 1637). This confirms the grandfather link already in vault.
- Consistent with vault note that widow petitioned 1726 (her husband died 1719, seven years widowed).

### 2. Mother: Anna Katarina Hoffman (1664-post-1726)
- Maiden name Hoffman, daughter of Hans Jost Hoffman & Christina Frank.
- Marriage 29 Jul 1684 Schriesheim to Johannes Michael Brecht.
- Name variant Anna Katharina = Anna Catherina (German vs. anglicized form).
- Baptism sponsor for Johann Michael Jr (1706) was **Hans Michael Hoffman**, likely her brother or close kin, corroborating the Hoffman connection.

### 3. Siblings of Johann Michael Brecht II (b. 1706)
Per Familypedia listing of 11 children of Johannes Michael Brecht (1662-1719):
- **Johannes Stephan Brecht** (1692-1747, WikiTree Brecht-10) — the "Stefan" who emigrated 1726 with mother and brother.
- **Anna Margaretha Durst** (born Brecht)
- **Appolonia Bender / Eichleberger** (born Brecht)
- Additional siblings listed in vault [[Johann_Michael_Brecht]] (Anna Elizabeth, Balthasar, Susanna Margaretha Roescher, Johann, Anna Katharina Dehuff, Janet Steele) pending line-by-line Schriesheim register verification.

### 4. Great-grandparents (already in vault)
The line extends: Johann Michael II (1706) → Johannes Michael (1662) → [[Balthasar_Hans_Brecht]] (1636-1703) → Kuntz/Conrad Brecht (1563-1612) per RootsWeb "Descendants of Kuntz Brecht" and Geni Conrad Kuntz Brecht 1563-1612 project.

## Caveats

- All confirming sources are Tier 3 user-contributed trees (Familypedia, WikiTree, Geni) that may be derived from a common earlier source (likely Green 1900 or Mills 1941, as cited in WikiTree Brecht-58). Two-independent-sources standard not strictly met.
- The 1726 destination detail "sailed to New York, settled first at Schoscharie" in one source contradicts the vault's "landed Philadelphia October 1726". This appears to be a confusion with brother Johannes Stephan's separate Schoharie connection. Flag for resolution but does NOT affect parent identification.
- Anna Katarina Hoffman's death date/place not recovered; she survived to at least May 1726 to petition for emigration.
