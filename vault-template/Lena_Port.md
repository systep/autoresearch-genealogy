## Biography

Lena was born **8 January 1892** in Kovno, Russian Empire, the daughter of [[Sarah_Salk]]. Her father is not named on the currently-available index record; given her mother's surname Salk and Lena's own surname Salk (inferred from the "Lena Salk → Lena Port" marriage pattern), her father was likely another member of the Salk/Zalk family in Kovno Gubernia (Salk endogamy is attested elsewhere in this family; [[Abraham_Zolly_Salk]]'s mother Rose Salk also carried a "Salk" maiden name).

On **20 December 1917** she married **[[Simon_Port]]** in Providence, Rhode Island. Simon was a Lithuanian-born man who had earlier lived in New Jersey with multiple children, suggesting he was a widower at the time of this marriage. Lena became his second wife. The couple's subsequent residence and descendants are documented in [[Simon_Port]].
