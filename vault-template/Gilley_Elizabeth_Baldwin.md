## Biography

Gilley Elizabeth Baldwin was born on 19 August 1805 in Greenville, South Carolina. She was the daughter of Eli Baldwin (1770 to 1840) and Edaline L. Transon (1783 to 1840). The Baldwin family was part of the Standing Springs community in Greenville County, South Carolina; her father Eli died in Standing Springs in 1840.

Gilley Elizabeth married Jeddiah W. Clark (b. 5 March 1801, South Carolina; d. 27 January 1860, Simpsonville, Greenville, SC). Together they had several children, including Sarah Elizabeth Clark and Eli Berry Clark (b. 31 July 1825, Greenville County; d. 26 November 1895, Greenville County).

Gilley Elizabeth survived her husband by 26 years, dying on 25 May 1886 in Simpsonville, Greenville, South Carolina.
