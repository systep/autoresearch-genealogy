## Biography

Georg Krauss was a Transylvanian Saxon of the Krauss/Krause family. He was the father of [[Johann_Krause_Binder]] (b. 1794). Stub record; minimal data available.

**Note on the "Krauss" surname**: In Transylvanian Saxon villages, many families shared the same surname. To distinguish them, a supplementary Beiname (by-name) was used. "Krauss" (meaning "curly haired") was likely the Beiname that distinguished this Binder family from other Binder families in the village. Thus Georg Krauss may have been "Georg Krauss Binder" or simply "Georg, of the Krauss (Binder) family." The fact that the GEDCOM records him as "Georg Krauss" without "Binder" may mean either that the Beiname had not yet been attached in his generation, or that the tree compiler dropped the second surname.
