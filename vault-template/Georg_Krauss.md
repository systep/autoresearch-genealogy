## Biography

Per the **MUTTER church certificate** (Tier 1, 4 April 1938, Pastor A. Bogeschdorfer), Georg Krauss (Alias Binder) was person (G): the earliest ancestor on [[Anna_Binder]]'s paternal line that could be traced in the Kreisch church registers. He was baptized **14 February 1794**, son of **Johannes** (no surname given for the father). His mother is not recorded in the registers.

The certificate notes that the Kreisch registers contain no further data beyond this point: "Sonstige Daten und Angaben finden sich in den Matrikeln in Kreisch nicht."

**Note on the "Krauss/Binder" surname**: In Transylvanian Saxon villages, many families shared the same surname. To distinguish them, a supplementary Beiname (by-name) was used. "Krauss" (meaning "curly haired") was likely the Beiname that distinguished this Binder family from other Binder families in the village. The MUTTER certificate uses "Georg Krauss (Alias Binder)," confirming that "Krauss" was his primary surname and "Binder" was an alias or family by-name. His son is recorded as "Johann Binder (eigentlich Krauss)," showing the names were used interchangeably.
