## Biography

Eleanor Sands was born on 5 October 1692 in Bensalem, Bucks County, Province of Pennsylvania. She was the daughter of Stephen Sands and Jane Cowgill. The Sands family was part of the colonial Quaker community in Bucks County, and the Sands/Cowgill/Hough network represents an interconnected group of early Pennsylvania settlers.

Stephen Sands (Eleanor's father) was the son of Richard Sands and Hester Sandys (born Aucher). Jane Cowgill (Eleanor's mother) was the daughter of Ralph Cowgill and Ellen Cowgill (born Stackhouse). Jane died on 26 November 1699 in Neshaminy, Bucks County, and was buried at Middletown Friends Cemetery.

Eleanor married John Hough III (b. 16 July 1682, Hough, Wilmslow Parish, Cheshire, England; d. 17 November 1744, Solebury, Bucks, PA). The user notes that the connection runs through the colonial Hough/Sands network. Together they had at least eight children.
