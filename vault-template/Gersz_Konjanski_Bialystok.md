## Biography

Gersz Konjanski appears in the JRI-Poland Białystok Jewish vital register death index as the subject of **record #138M, 1874** (microfilm LDS 1191936), his own death record, naming his father as **Srol** ([[Srol_Konjanski_Bialystok]]). See [[Kuniansky_JRI_Poland_Bialystok_2026_04_19]] for the full derivation and context.

He is also the plausible father of **Sore Leja Kunjanska** ([[Sore_Leja_Kunjanska_Bialystok]]), whose 1866 Białystok death record (akta #103F, microfilm LDS 1191934) names her father simply as "Gersz." The inference that this is the same Gersz rests on shared surname, shared town, and chronological consistency (Sore Leja d. 1866 while Gersz lived to 1874), but is not explicitly proven without the full register images.

**Relationship to the vault's Atlanta Kuniansky cluster**: originally held as a speculative candidate ancestor / great-grandfather of [[Wolf_Kuniansky]], **downgraded to unlikely on 2026-04-19** after independent sources placed the Atlanta cluster's origin in Podolia Gubernia / Vinnytsia region (Ukraine), not Grodno Gubernia / Białystok. See [[Kuniansky_JRI_Poland_Bialystok_2026_04_19]] for the reassessment.

Retained as a stub person file to preserve the evidence of a distinct Białystok Konjanski family.
