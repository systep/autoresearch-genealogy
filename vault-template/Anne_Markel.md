## Biography

Anne Markel was the second of five children of [[Morris_Markel]] and Helen Brandt Markel. She was likely born c. 1922 in Galicia (Sambor district, Poland after 1918), as the family emigrated to the United States c. 1928 when her younger sister Kate was age 3. She is documented in the 1940 and 1950 US Censuses living in the Morris Markel household at 28 Harris Avenue (1940) and 12 Capron (1950) in West Warwick, Rhode Island, alongside her parents and siblings. Her mother's 1976 obituary lists Anne among the five surviving children (Tier 2).

She is identified in the family-archive group portrait (chat-shared 2026-04-25) by [[Markel_Family_Portrait_Identification_Key]] bottom-row #13 ("Anne Markel").
