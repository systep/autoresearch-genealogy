## Biography

Chaya Mushka Shneuri was a daughter of [[Dovber_Shneuri]] (the Mitteler Rebbe, 2nd Lubavitcher Rebbe) and the wife of [[Menachem_Mendel_Schneerson_Tzemach_Tzedek]] (the Tzemach Tzedek, 3rd Lubavitcher Rebbe, 1789-1866) (GEDCOM, unverified).

She was a sister of [[Rivka_Beila_Schneerson]] (1797), [[Menucha_Rachel_Shneuri]] (1798), [[Chaya_Sarah_Shneuri]] (1801), and others (unverified).

Through her husband, she was the mother of [[Shmuel_Schneerson]] (Maharash, 4th Rebbe), [[Yehuda_Leib_Schneerson]], [[Chaim_Shneur_Zalman_Schneerson]], [[Israel_Noah_Schneerson]], [[Yaakov_Schneerson]], and [[Dvorah_Leah_Schneerson]] (unverified).
