## Biography

Rabbi Levi Yitzchok Zalmanson was born in 1812 (GEDCOM, unverified). He served as Av Beis Din. He married Dvorah Leah Schneursohn, daughter of the 3rd Lubavitcher Rebbe.

He was the brother of [[Srul_Mendel_Zalmanson]] (1820-1854), whose son [[Levi_Itzhak_Zalmanson]] (b. 1851) was the father of [[Barnett_Salmanson]]. Thus Rabbi Levi Yitzchok was Barnett's great-uncle.

**Important**: This is NOT [[Levi_Itzhak_Zalmanson]] (b. 1851), Barnett's father. That Levi Itzhak was named after this uncle.

He died March 5, 1872 in Vitebsk, Belarus.
