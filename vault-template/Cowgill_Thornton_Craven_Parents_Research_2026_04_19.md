# Hypothesis: Cowgill Pedigree Back to Bryan Cowgill of Thornton in Craven

## Statement

Round 9 established that **Edmund Cowgill of Thornton in Craven (baptized 23 Nov 1634)**, not Ralph Cowgill, was the husband of Ellin (Stackhouse) Cowgill and the father of the Lamb-1682 Cowgill emigrants (John, Edmund, Jane, Jennett) and of Ralph Cowgill who preceded them on Friends Adventure.

This hypothesis extends the Cowgill line one generation further back:

1. **Edmund Cowgill Sr.** (bap. 7 Feb 1601/2 at St Mary's, Carleton in Craven; bur. 15 Dec 1642 at Thornton in Craven; m. 30 Jun 1631 Jenet Rideogh at Thornton in Craven) is the father of Edmund Jr. (b. 1634).
2. **John Cowgill of Lothersdale** (bap. 27 Sep 1572 at Thornton in Craven; bur. 23 Jul 1634 at St Mary's, Carleton in Craven; m. Margaret Gardiner 3 May 1601 at Thornton in Craven; m. 2nd Mary/Maria Crosley 4 Jun 1634 at Colne) is the father of Edmund Sr.
3. **Bryan Cowgill** (of Thornton in Craven, first attested in parish records 1567) is the father of John of Lothersdale, placing the family firmly in the West Riding by the mid-16th century.
4. **Jenet Rideogh** (m. Edmund Sr. 1631) is the mother of Edmund Jr. (b. 1634). Her maiden family ("Rideogh") is a Yorkshire surname that warrants a separate search. She is probably the "widow Gennett Cowgill" named in Robert Cowgill's 1652 will.

## Evidence For

1. **Cowgill Family Association, John Cowgill of Lothersdale Family Tree** (cowgillcousins.org/johnoflothersdale.html): names John Cowgill 1572-1634, wife Margaret Gardiner, and children including Edmund 1601/2-1642. States that Edmund married Jenet Rideogh 30 Jun 1631 at Thornton in Craven and that their son Edmund Jr. was baptized 23 Nov 1634. Tier 2.
2. **Cowgill Family Association, Edmund Cowgill Family Tree** (cowgillcousins.org/edmundcowgill.html): covers Edmund Jr. (b. 1634) and his American descendants. Tier 2.
3. **Cowgill Family Association, Bryan Cowgill Family Tree** (cowgillcousins.org/bryancowgill.html): documents Bryan Cowgill as John's father and traces the family into the 16th century. Tier 2.
4. **Cowgill Family Association, Robert Cowgill Family Tree** (cowgillcousins.org/robertcowgill.html): documents Robert Cowgill's 1652 will and the bequest to "widow Gennett Cowgill," providing cross-corroboration of Jenet (Rideogh) Cowgill's widowhood after Edmund Sr.'s 1642 burial. Tier 2.
5. **Thornton in Craven parish-record series**: Cowgills attested continuously from 1543 (Robert on Lay Subsidy Roll) through 1567 (Bryan), 1572 (John bap.), 1601 (John m. Margaret Gardiner), 1631 (Edmund m. Jenet Rideogh), 1634 (Edmund Jr. bap.), 1642 (Edmund Sr. bur.). Tier 1 series, accessed via Cowgill Family Association abstracts.

## Evidence Against

1. Independent confirmation (other than Cowgill Family Association) not yet obtained in web-accessible sources. Geni entry for Edmund Cowgill 1602-1642 contains internally conflicting child data suggesting it conflates two Edmund Cowgills of overlapping period.
2. Original parish register images not viewed.
3. "Rideogh" as a surname is unusual; a search on the surname itself is warranted to verify the spelling and locate Jenet's baptism and parentage.

## Analysis

The Cowgill Family Association presents a coherent five-generation reconstruction from Bryan Cowgill (fl. 1567) down to Ellin (Stackhouse) Cowgill's children. All dates, locations, and naming patterns are internally consistent with Thornton in Craven / Carleton in Craven / Lothersdale parish activity. The association explicitly cites the parish registers as its underlying source. This is Moderate Signal. It upgrades to Strong Signal once any one of the register entries is independently verified (FamilySearch digitised microfilm or a West Yorkshire Archive Service lookup).

The line of descent from Round 9 now extends:

**Bryan Cowgill (fl. 1567, Thornton in Craven) → John Cowgill of Lothersdale (1572-1634) + Margaret Gardiner → Edmund Cowgill Sr. (1601/2-1642) + Jenet Rideogh → Edmund Cowgill Jr. (bap. 1634) + Ellin Stackhouse → children on Lamb 1682 (and Ralph on Friends Adventure).**

## Decisive Record

- **For Edmund Sr.'s parentage**: Carleton in Craven parish register baptism entry 7 Feb 1601/2 naming John and Margaret (Gardiner) Cowgill as parents.
- **For Edmund Sr.'s marriage**: Thornton in Craven parish register marriage entry 30 Jun 1631, Edmund Cowgill and Jenet Rideogh.
- **For Jenet's widowhood**: Robert Cowgill's 1652 will (probate copy, Borthwick Institute) naming widow Gennett Cowgill.
- **For Bryan Cowgill's parentage and lifespan**: Thornton in Craven parish register entries from 1567 onward; additional 16th-century sources (Bryan of James Family Tree on cowgillcousins.org suggests an even earlier generation).
