# Edward Moseley of Kinver (bp. 1599, d. 1673/74)

## Summary

Edward Moseley, baptized St Peter's parish church, Kinver (then spelled Kinfare), Staffordshire, on 25 April 1599; died Kinver, early 1673/74. Father of Capt William Moseley (1630-1676/81), Virginia immigrant of 1650 and Burgess of Rappahannock County.

He lived in London circa 1612 to 1617, then returned to Whittington (a hamlet in Kinver parish) or Kinfare itself for the remainder of his life.

## Key Facts

### Birth and Baptism

- Baptized St Peter's, Kinver, 25 April 1599.
- Sixth of six known children of Edward Moseley Sr. (bp. 1564/65) and Elizabeth (Brettell) Moseley.

### Marriage

- Married Margaret (maiden name not yet established), probably at Kinver or Whittington, early 1620s.

### Residence

- Whittington and Kinfare, Staffordshire, for most of adult life.
- London circa 1612 to 1617 (apprenticeship or trade, per WikiTree Kinfare space).

### Death

- Died Kinfare, early 1673/74 per WikiTree synthesis of parish register and manorial records.
