## Biography

Mary Elizabeth Huff was born June 21, 1838 in South Carolina, the daughter of **Lewis P Huff** (b. July 22, 1803) and **Mary Brashier**. The Huff family's origins trace to Southside Virginia (Brunswick/Bedford Counties), with both Kentucky and South Carolina Huff branches sharing this Virginia origin. The South Carolina Huff connection runs through Philemon Huff (1763-1843, VA to SC), who married into the Payne family; the Paynes migrated with the Coxes from North Carolina to South Carolina in the 1780s.

She married **William Thomas Long** (Nov 25, 1825 - 1887) on **January 19, 1857**, with the ceremony performed by **Rev. K. Callard** (per handwritten family chart).

**1860 Census:** Elizabeth Long appears in Madison County, Kentucky, age 22, with husband William Long. This places the couple in Kentucky early in their marriage.

Their headstone in the Standing Springs area reads: "William T. Long 1825-1887 / Elizabeth Huff__ 18?0-1922" (the final digits of her birth year are partially illegible on the stone).

Mary Elizabeth and William Thomas had at least two sons: **William Goldsmith Long** (1862-1937), who married Roxanne Cox (1865-1912), and **James Louis Long**. Through William Goldsmith, she is an ancestor of Rebecca Moseley Long (1898-1985), Mary Elizabeth Bright, Kathryn Ann Kascher, and the Salmanson family.

Mary Elizabeth survived her husband by 35 years, dying August 6, 1922 at age 84.

**Note on the "Huff" surname:** The Huff name also appears in connection with Marcia (Mary) Kopp/Huff, who married William I. King. IMG_0786 records "Kopp" and IMG_0792 records "Huff" for the same person. This suggests the Huff family was part of the intermarried South Carolina cluster. See Family Tree Discrepancy #5.
