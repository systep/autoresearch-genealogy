## Biography

Paul Christman was born in 1605 in Mannheim, Germany. He held the rank of Commander (Kurfurstlicher Grenadierhauptmann). He was a Protestant. He married Anna Maria Herring and was the father of [[Anna_Margaretha_Christmann]] (b. 1 Jan 1637), who married [[Balthasar_Hans_Brecht]]. He also had a son Wilhelm Christmann, who served as a Kurfurstlicher Grenadierhauptmann. Paul died in 1666 in Schriesheim.
