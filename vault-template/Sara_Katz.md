## Biography

Sara Katz was born in 1665 in Poznan, Poland (GEDCOM, Filae). She was descended from Isaac Katz, who was the Maharal's son-in-law (user-provided data).

Her father was [[Shneur_Zalman_Katz]] and her mother was [[Rosa_Luria]] (Filae record 14100:2436013835). However, both of these parental identifications are UNVERIFIED.

Sara married [[Moshe_Loew_of_Posen]] (Moshe Loew of Posen, 1663-1736, rabbi and Rav of Posna) (GEDCOM). Their children included Freidel Loew (born 1695, Poznan) and [[Schneur_Zalman_Loew]], who became the paternal grandfather of the Alter Rebbe.

Sara died in 1736 at approximately age 70 to 71 (GEDCOM).
