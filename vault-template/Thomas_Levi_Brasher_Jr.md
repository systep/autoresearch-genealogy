## Biography

Thomas Levi Brasher Jr. was born 18 June 1731 in Baltimore County, Maryland, son of Thomas Brasher Sr. (bap. 1701 St. George's Parish Baltimore, d. ~1770 Orange Co NC) and Sarah (Constance). The 2026-04-22 vault Research_Log notes that "the Brasher chain extends to Thomas Brasher Sr. (bap. 1701 St. George's Parish Baltimore MD, Tier 1 church register, d. 1770 Orange Co NC) → William Brasher (c.1674 Baltimore) + Elizabeth Clayton; the Huguenot Brasseur connection is REFUTED."

Thomas Jr. married Sarah Lindsey about 17 May 1770 in Greenville, South Carolina. He died c. 1789 in Greenville County, SC.

His daughter [[Elizabeth_Brasher_Henderson|Elizabeth Brasher]] (~1764-1839) married [[Ezekiel_Henderson]] (1763-1846) on 17 October 1782 in Chatham County, NC -- placing Thomas Jr. as the great-grandfather of [[Olive_Mary_Polly_Henderson_Cox]] (1795-1876, wife of [[Thomas_B_Cox]]).

Thomas Jr.'s son **Thomas Levi Brasher III (1765-1841)** is the strongest candidate father for [[Mary_Brashier]] (b. 1806), wife of [[Lewis_P_Huff]] (per vault [[Mary_Brashier]] §parentage hypothesis). If confirmed, this would make Thomas Jr. the great-great-great-grandfather of [[William_Goldsmith_Long]] (1862-1937) via two independent kinship paths (via Elizabeth + Ezekiel + Olive Mary + Thomas B. Cox + Thomas Manning Cox + Roxanne Cox + William Goldsmith Long, AND via Thomas III + Mary Brashier + Lewis P. Huff + Mary Elizabeth Huff + William Goldsmith Long's mother).
