## Biography

Henriette Goldberg is known only from the 24 November 1910 New York marriage certificate of her daughter [[Rebecca_Marcus_Friedberg]], which records Henriette as the bride's mother by her maiden name. She married [[Willy_Friedberg]], and their daughter Rebecca was born circa 1879 in Libau (Liepāja, Latvia). Henriette's birthplace is listed as Russia; by context (daughter born in Libau), she likely lived in the Kurland governorate.

**Witness link**: Bernhard Goldberg, a witness at Rebecca's 1910 NY wedding, is almost certainly a maternal relative (probable brother or cousin of Henriette), though the relationship is not explicitly stated.

### Middle name/initial ambiguity

The marriage certificate appears to list an additional initial or middle name for Henriette that is partially illegible — possibly "L." (Leah?). A higher-resolution image review or retrieval of the original certificate would clarify.
