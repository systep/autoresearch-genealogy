## Biography

Martha Estelle Salmanson was born July 1, 1918 (GEDCOM, unverified) in Rhode Island, the daughter of Barnett and Elizabeth (Salk) Salmanson. She was the youngest daughter and fourth of five children; her brothers were Leonard Irving (1910-1975), Samuel (1912-2004), Charles (1914-2008), and Donald (1924-2015).

Martha graduated from **Central High School** in Providence.

She married first **Paul Corin** and had a daughter, Rosalind (later married Herbert Hill). She later married **Edward Schwartz** and had a son, Larry Schwartz (who married Maxine).

Martha was a member of **Temple Emanu-El** and **Hadassah** in Providence.

She died September 26, 2015 at age 95. At the time of her death, she was survived by her brother Donald Salmanson (who himself died later in 2015).
