## Biography

Identity of Ralph Cowgill is disputed. Two competing reconstructions exist:

1. **Ralph as husband of Ellin Stackhouse** (user-provided data; WikiTree Cowgill-16; Geni): Ralph Cowgill (c.1635-1682) married Ellin Stackhouse and died in England before the Lamb sailed. This is the common online-tree identification but is treated as Tier 3 (user-contributed) and is contradicted by the Cowgill Family Association.
2. **Ralph as son of Edmund and Ellin Cowgill** (Cowgill Family Association, cowgillcousins.org): Ralph was a son who sailed ahead of his mother on the ship Friends Adventure in May 1682, while Ellin followed on the Lamb in October 1682 with her other children (John, Edmund, Jane, Jennett). Under this reconstruction, Ellin's actual husband was Edmund Cowgill of Thornton in Craven (baptized 23 Nov 1634), who died in England before June 1682.

See [[Cowgill_Hayhurst_Quaker_Research_2026_04_19]] for the full analysis. Current weight of evidence favors reconstruction (2): Ralph is Ellin's son, not her husband.
