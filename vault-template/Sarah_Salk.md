## Biography

Sarah Salk was born in June 1869 in Lithuania/Russia (GEDCOM, unverified), the daughter of [[Louis_Elizar_Salk]] and [[Rose_Salk]]. She was a sister of [[Abraham_Zolly_Salk]] (father of [[Elizabeth_Salk]]) and [[Hyman_Salk_1873]]. She appears to be the eldest of the known children.

### Confirmed daughter: Lena (Salk) Port

The Rhode Island Marriages 1850-1922 index (Tier 2) records **Sarah Salk as the mother of Lena (Salk) Port**, who married [[Simon_Port]] in Providence on 20 December 1917. Lena was born **8 January 1892 in Kovno, Russia**, placing her birth in Kovno Gubernia while Sarah was there (before Sarah's eventual emigration to Rhode Island). See [[Lena_Port]]. This adds a previously-unknown daughter to Sarah's vault record and elevates her source count from single Tier 3 to Tier 3 + Tier 2.

The 1892 Kovno birthplace of Lena confirms that Sarah remained in Kovno through at least the early 1890s. Sarah's eventual arrival in Providence (where she died 1924) likely followed either [[Abraham_Zolly_Salk]] (arrived ~1894-1896) or later, with Lena following separately and marrying Simon Port in 1917.

Sarah died September 21, 1924 in Providence, Rhode Island, approximately five months before her brother Abraham died (February 22, 1925).
