## Biography

Anna Katharina Hoffman was born on 27 September 1663 (some records say 14 Oct 1664) in Schriesheim, Baden-Wuerttemberg, Germany. She was the daughter of [[Hans_Jost_Hoffman]], a councilman, and [[Christina_Frank]]. On 29 July 1684, she married [[Johann_Michael_Brecht]] (b. 12 Oct 1662, d. 11 Aug 1719), a government official in Schriesheim.

After her husband's death in 1719, Anna Katharina continued to manage the family's vineyard and tavern interests. On 6 May 1726, she petitioned with her sons Stefan and Johann (Michael) to emigrate to America. They landed in Philadelphia in 1727, settling first in Germantown before moving to Heidelberg Township, Lancaster County, Pennsylvania before April 1728. She died on 10 October 1740 in Berks County, Pennsylvania and was buried at Trinity Church Cemetery in Reading.

She had at least 10 children, many of whom established prominent families in colonial Pennsylvania. Her siblings included Adam Hoffman, Johannes Hoffman, Hans Michael Hoffman, Hans Philip Hoffman, and Valentin Hoffman.
