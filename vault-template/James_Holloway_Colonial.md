## Biography

James Holloway (circa 1635, 1676) was an early Virginia settler residing in Charles River Parish, York County, Virginia. He was the father of David Holloway (1664, 1732).

His wife's first name was Ann, but her maiden surname is uncertain. "Waller" has appeared in some genealogical compilations but is unconfirmed by primary records.

James died in 1676. His son David Holloway later married Elizabeth Mathews (daughter of [[John_Mathews]] and granddaughter of [[Governor_Samuel_Mathews]]), connecting the Holloway line to Virginia's colonial elite.

Through David Holloway's descendants, this line connects to [[Martha_Patty_Holloway]] and the broader Moseley/Huff family network in the vault.
