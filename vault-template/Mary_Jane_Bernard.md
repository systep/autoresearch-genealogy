## Biography

Mary Jane Bernard was born on August 13, 1829 in Washington, Virginia (GEDCOM). She married [[Samuel_Graham_Bright]] on April 17, 1851 in Franklin County, Virginia (GEDCOM).

Census records place the couple in Franklin and Nicholas, Virginia in 1860, Rocky Mount, Franklin, Virginia in 1870 and 1880, and Rocky Mount District, Franklin, Virginia in 1900 (GEDCOM).

Her daughter Mary Emma (later Walker) is confirmed by Find a Grave memorial #38926360. Mary Jane and Samuel had nine known children together.

Mary Jane died on September 15, 1900 in Rocky Mount, Franklin, Virginia and was buried there (GEDCOM).
