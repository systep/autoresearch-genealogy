## Biography

Samuel Zipperman was born March 18, 1888 in Warsaw, Poland (GEDCOM, unverified). He worked as a tile mason and clerk. He married [[Anna_Salk_Zipperman]], a daughter of [[Abraham_Zolly_Salk]] and [[Tauba_Salk]]. Through his wife Anna, Samuel was a brother in law of [[Elizabeth_Salk]] (who married [[Barnett_Salmanson]]).

The family settled in Atlanta, Georgia. Their children included Louis (~1915-2007), Bette (1918-2017), Shirley (1922-1987), and Arnold (b. 1929).

Samuel died March 21, 1977 in Atlanta at approximately age 89.
