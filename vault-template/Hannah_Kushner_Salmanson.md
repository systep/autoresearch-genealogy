## Biography

Hannah Kushner was born June 26, 1914 in Providence, Rhode Island, the daughter of Jacob Kushner and Dora (Alpert) Kushner. Jacob was born January 27, 1886 in Russia and arrived in the United States in 1890 or 1895 (multiple arrival records exist). The family resided in Providence Ward 3 (1910 Census).

Hannah had three siblings: Esta (Kushner) Katz of Providence, Samuel Kushner (deceased by the time of Hannah's death), and Phillip Kushner (also deceased).

She married **Charles Salmanson** (1914-2008), the third son of Barnett and Elizabeth (Salk) Salmanson. They had two children: **Jerrold A. Salmanson** (b. January 30, 1942, Providence, RI; d. 2024) and **Deborah Salmanson Roberts**.

Jerrold worked in the family Adams Drug / Brooks Drug business alongside his cousin Alan C. Salmanson. Samuel Salmanson's 2004 obituary refers to "his nephews, Jerrold and Alan," confirming Jerrold was Charles's son (Samuel's nephew), not Leonard's.

Hannah died December 4, 2001 at the age of 87.
