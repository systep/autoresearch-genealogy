## Biography

Hans Jost Hoffman was born in 1628 in Schriesheim, Baden, Germany. He was the son of Engehard Hoffman. He worked as a Maurergesell (journeyman bricklayer) as of 1651, and later served as Ratsverwandter (councilman) between 1684 and 1697.

He married first [[Christina_Frank]] on 15 April 1651 in Schriesheim. After Christina's death on 2 April 1685, he married Appolonia Krag on 29 September 1685.

His daughter [[Anna_Katherine_Hoffman]] (b. 1663) married [[Johann_Michael_Brecht]] and later emigrated to colonial Pennsylvania. Hans Jost died on 26 February 1697 in Schriesheim and was buried there.

Also known by name variants: Hans Joost Hoffman, Hans Yost Hoffman, Hans Jakob Hoffmann, Chancellor Hans Yost Hoffman.
