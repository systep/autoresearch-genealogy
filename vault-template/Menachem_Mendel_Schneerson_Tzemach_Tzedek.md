## Biography

Rabbi Menachem Mendel Schneerson, known as the **Tzemach Tzedek**, was the third Rebbe of the Chabad-Lubavitch Hasidic dynasty (GEDCOM, unverified). The GEDCOM records his birth on September 9, 1789, in Liozna (also given as Leozna/Lizna), Belarus, and his death on March 17, 1866, in Lyubavichi, Smolensk, Russia (unverified).

His father was Shalom Shachne Altschuler (born Schneerson, 1772-1827), and his mother was [[Deborah_Leah_Shneuri]] (1766-1792), a daughter of [[Shneur_Zalman_of_Liadi]] (the Alter Rebbe) (GEDCOM, unverified). His mother died when he was approximately three years old, and he was raised by his grandfather, the Alter Rebbe.

He married [[Chaya_Mushka_Shneuri]] (1789-1861), a daughter of [[Dovber_Shneuri]] (the Mitteler Rebbe, 2nd Lubavitcher Rebbe), making him both a grandson and son-in-law within the Schneerson dynasty (GEDCOM, unverified).

The GEDCOM contains multiple entries for this person (at least three duplicates with the same biographical data), reflecting parallel Smart Matches from different source trees. Note: GEDCOM dates show minor discrepancies: some entries give birth as "20 SEP 1789" while others give "9 SEP 1789" (unverified).

### Connection to the Zalmanson/Salmanson Line

His daughter [[Dvorah_Leah_Schneerson]] married Rabbi [[Levi_Yitzchok_Zalmanson]] (1812-1872), son of [[Yekutiel_Zalman_Wallis]] and [[Rivka_Beila_Schneerson]]. This means Rabbi Levi Yitzchok Zalmanson was both his son-in-law and his wife's nephew (since Rivka Beila was the Mitteler Rebbe's daughter) (unverified).
