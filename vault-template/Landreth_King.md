## Biography

Landreth King was born about 1880 in South Carolina, a son of [[Judson_King]] (1841-1921) and [[Charstee_King]] (Charstee A. Cooley, 1842-1921). He appears in the 1900 Census as "Landrath King," age 19, living in his parents' household in Oaklawn Township Ward 1, Greenville County, SC (FamilySearch ARK M3TM-NCY; see [[Judson_King]] for the full 1900 enumeration). He was not among the seven children documented in the 1880 census, so he was born between 1880 and 1882.

By 1910 he was a married head of household in Oaklawn, Greenville County, with wife **Polly E King** and sons **Marvin E King** and **Roy L King**. He remained in the same township as his parents. The 1910 Census indexes his given name as "Landreth," which is likely the correct spelling; "Landrath" on the 1900 record appears to be an indexer error.
