## Biography

Martha "Patty" Holloway was born c. 1755 in Brunswick County, Virginia. The pedigree chart identifies her father as **William Holloway** (b. 24 Dec 1734, York VA; d. 22 Nov 1784, Mecklenburg VA). A William Holloway of Brunswick County left a will dated 16 October 1784 (Brunswick County Will Book 2, pp. 422-23) naming wife Martha, lending the plantation to her until death or remarriage, and dividing the estate among six living children and the children of a son Obed: sons Dan, Jesse, Asa, and Caleb, with executors Dan, Jesse, and Asa Holloway. The will was witnessed by **William Moseley Jr.**, Harmon Read, and Littleberry Pearson. An account of estate sale dated 1 April 1789 after the death of Martha (the wife named in the will) indicates Martha senior died c. 1789.

**Important note on identity:** The William Holloway who wrote the 1784 will and the William Holloway on the pedigree chart may or may not be the same person. The pedigree chart gives a death place of "Mecklenburg VA," while the will was probated in Brunswick County. Web research notes that there were at least two William Holloways in the area at this time, and they have been conflated in some genealogies. If Martha "Patty" Holloway (who married Samuel Moseley) was the daughter of the William Holloway who wrote the 1784 will, she would be one of the unnamed daughters (the will mentions only sons by name). The connection is plausible given that William Moseley Jr. witnessed the will, linking the Holloway and Moseley families directly.

WikiTree profile Holloway-58 identifies Martha as the mother of Rebecca (Moseley) Huff, Samuel Moseley, Huldah (Moseley) Huff, Abigail (Moseley) Holloway, and Amy Elizabeth (Moseley) Lynch.

Martha married [[Samuel_Moseley]] c. 1771 in Brunswick County, Virginia (WikiTree Holloway-58). Their daughter Abigail married Silas Holloway (son of Obediah Holloway) in 1799, creating another Moseley-Holloway intermarriage.

Through the [[James_Holloway_Colonial]] line, this Holloway family traces back to early colonial Virginia. James Holloway (c. 1635-1676) of Charles River Parish, York County VA had a son David Holloway (1664-1732) who married Elizabeth Mathews (granddaughter of Governor Samuel Mathews). The line from David to William Holloway (Martha's father) requires additional intermediate generations.

Martha died c. 1811 in Brunswick, Virginia (WikiTree Holloway-58).
