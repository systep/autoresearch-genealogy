## Biography

Jeddiah W. Clark was born on 5 March 1801 in South Carolina. He was the son of James Hiriam Clark. He married [[Gilley_Elizabeth_Baldwin]] (b. 19 Aug 1805, d. 25 May 1886) and was the father of [[Sarah_Elizabeth_Clark]] (b. 13 Aug 1836, d. 5 Jul 1879), who married [[Thomas_Manning_Cox]].
