## Biography

Rabbi Levi Yitzchok Zalmanson was the eldest son of [[Yekutiel_Zalman_Wallis]] and [[Rivka_Beila_Schneerson]] (daughter of the Mitteler Rebbe) (GEDCOM, unverified). He served as Av Beis Din (unverified).

He married [[Dvorah_Leah_Schneerson]], the eldest daughter of the Tzemach Tzedek, in 1826. This created a double connection to the Schneerson dynasty (unverified).

**Note:** This is NOT the same person as [[Levi_Itzhak_Zalmanson]] (1851-~1907), who was his nephew (son of his brother [[Srul_Mendel_Zalmanson]]) and the grandfather of [[Barnett_Salmanson]].
