## Biography

Christina Frank was born in 1626 in Mannheim, Germany, and baptized on 24 November 1626 in Walheim, Wuerttemberg. She was the daughter of Hans Frank (Franck). Her siblings included Hans Wendel Frank, Anna Margaretha Frank, Hans Georg Frank, and Hans Valentin Frank.

On 15 April 1651, she married [[Hans_Jost_Hoffman]], a journeyman bricklayer and later councilman in Schriesheim. Their daughter [[Anna_Katherine_Hoffman]] (b. 1663) married [[Johann_Michael_Brecht]] and emigrated to colonial Pennsylvania. Christina died on 2 April 1685 in Schriesheim.

Also known as: Christina Krank, Christina Franckin, Mrs. Hans Yost Hoffman.
