## Biography

John Baldwin Jr. was born in 1697 in Chester County, Pennsylvania. He was the son of [[John_Baldwin_Oxfordshire|John Baldwin Sr.]], one of the three Baldwin brothers who immigrated from Oxfordshire, England in 1682.

John Jr. worked as a saddler. On April 11, 1719, he married Hannah Johnson, as recorded in the Chester Monthly Meeting Records (Tier 1).

John Jr. and Hannah had at least two sons, John and Joshua, who are mentioned in the 1732 will of their grandfather John Baldwin Sr. (Chester County probate records, Tier 1). This will provides the key evidence linking John Jr. as the son of John Sr. and the father of these two grandsons.

John Jr. died before 1745 in Chester County, Pennsylvania.
