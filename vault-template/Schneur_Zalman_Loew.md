## Biography

Rabbi Schneur Zalman Loew served as a rabbi in Poznan (GEDCOM). He was the paternal grandfather of the Alter Rebbe, [[Shneur_Zalman_of_Liadi]], founder of Chabad-Lubavitch. The Alter Rebbe was named after him.

He married Rachel Portugaler (~1693-~1735), whose father was Boruch Portugaler/Batlan (b. 1670) (Family Tree). Both Schneur Zalman and Rachel appear to have died in the same year (~1735), when their son [[Boruch_Poisner]] was only about 14 years old.

The GEDCOM contains two separate entries for Schneur Zalman Loew (I500972 and I500975), both with the same dates and location, reflecting parallel Smart Match confirmations from different source trees.

His daughter Devorah Leah (b. 1719, Poznan) married into the Kerpil family (GEDCOM).
