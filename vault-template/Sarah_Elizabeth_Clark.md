## Biography

Sarah Elizabeth Clark, known as "Lizzie," was born on August 13, 1836 in Greenville County, South Carolina, to Jeddiah W. Clark and Gilley Elizabeth Baldwin (GEDCOM). She married Thomas Manning Cox, a deacon of Standing Springs Baptist Church in Simpsonville. Together they had eight known children spanning from approximately 1854 to 1869 (GEDCOM).

Sarah died on July 5, 1879 at the age of 42 (GEDCOM). She was buried at Standing Springs Cemetery in Simpsonville, South Carolina. Her headstone is recorded in both Find a Grave (#8304975) and BillionGraves. Notably, her son William L. Cox died just over a month after her, on August 14, 1879, at age 18 (GEDCOM).

Her daughter Roxanne Cox married William Goldsmith Long, continuing the Cox/Long intermarriage that links this colonial SC line to the present family (Family_Tree.md).
