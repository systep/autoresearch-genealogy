## Biography

Nachman Luria appears in the GEDCOM as a member of the Luria family, part of the claimed chain of descent from King David through the Exilarchs and Geonim. He is the son of [[Yoseph_Luria]] and father of [[Kalman_Luria]] (Kalonymos). The estimated birth date of 1230 places him in the 13th century, but no independent historical documentation has been identified for this individual.

The Luria family tradition claims descent from the French/German Ashkenazi scholarly elite, with origins traced back through the ibn Nagrela family to the Babylonian Geonim. The chain passes: Laemel > Yechezkel > Azariah ben Avraham > Avraham ben Yekhiel > Yechiel (grandson of Hai Gaon) > through the ha-Nagid line.
