## Biography

Chawa Frei Lockspeiser is a previously-undocumented sister of [[Leah_Frei]]. She is identified in the new (2026-04-25 ingested) family document `Assets/Lockspeisers-Mom's Maternal Aunt's Family.pdf` whose two pages contain:

- **Page 1 (front)**: studio photograph of 6 children in matching dark sailor suits with white collars, ages roughly 4 to 10. Studio signature/stamp at lower right (illegible at current resolution, possibly a Polish/Galician studio name).
- **Page 1 (back)**: Two distinct handwritings. Earlier (pencil, German cursive): "Zum Andenken / Jahre 1936" ("As a memento / Year 1936"), period inscription. Later (blue ink, English): "Tante Chava's / Children", added by an American descendant identifying the photo, presumably after it was sent to relatives in the United States.
- **Page 2 (front)**: portrait (head and shoulders) of a bearded man, dark hair, c. 30-40 years old.
- **Page 2 (back)**: blue ink, English: "Tante Chawa's / husband". Sideways down the left edge: "Hersch Lockspeiser" + a number "192_" or "1[??]" (possibly a date or catalog number, illegible).

The PDF file title supplied by the family is "Lockspeisers — Mom's Maternal Aunt's Family." In context, "Mom" = [[Florence_Markel_Mermelstein]] (Sura Feiga, 1909-1990), whose mother is [[Leah_Frei]]. Therefore "Mom's Maternal Aunt" = a sister of Leah Frei. This identifies Chawa as a fourth Frei sister, in addition to the three (Bashe, Feiga, Frieda) already recorded in the Yichus narrative by Ruth Gold (Oct 9, 2011, Tier 2).

She married **Hersch Lockspeiser** (see [[Hersch_Lockspeiser]]) and they had at least 6 children pictured in 1936. The 1936 inscription "Zum Andenken" (German for "as a memento") is the inscription pattern typically used when a photograph is sent as a keepsake to family abroad. This is consistent with the photo being sent from Chawa's family in interwar Poland (Galicia / Lwów region) to Florence Markel Mermelstein in Rhode Island.

**Confidence: Low.** Single Tier 2 source (the photo with handwritten captions). ## Open Questions

- What is Chawa's birth year? (Required to confirm she is plausibly Leah's sister and to locate vital records.)
- Did the family survive WWII? Lemberg / Lwów / Sambor region Jewish communities were systematically destroyed 1941-1943. The 1936 photo from the family was almost certainly sent before that destruction. Search Sambor Yizkor Book and Yad Vashem for "Lockspeiser" surname.
- Names of the 6 children pictured in 1936?
- Is Chawa among the four Frei sisters mentioned in the Yichus, but the Yichus mistakenly omitted her (perhaps deliberately, if she had a difficult relationship with the family) or used a different name for her?
- Did Chawa or Hersch's family emigrate? The photo's transmission to Florence in the US suggests a postal connection but not necessarily a personal emigration.
