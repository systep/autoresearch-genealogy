## Biography

Larkin Long was born about 1801 in South Carolina. He married Sarah S. (b. 1800, South Carolina), whose maiden name is not recorded in the GEDCOM.

Larkin and Sarah had at least one son, William Thomas Long (b. 25 November 1825, Simpsonville, Greenville, South Carolina; d. 16 October 1887, Simpsonville). The connection between Larkin and William Thomas Long is supported by a Geni World Family Tree record (via the entry for William Thomas Long) and by handwritten notes referenced in the user's research.

William Thomas Long married Mary Elizabeth Huff and had at least one child, James Louis Long.

The Geni record for William Thomas Long states his mother was "Sarah 'Sally' Long (born Goldsmith)," which suggests Sarah S.'s maiden name may have been Goldsmith. This remains (unverified).

Note: MyHeritage shows a later Larken Long (b. 1877) who is likely a descendant, not the same individual.
