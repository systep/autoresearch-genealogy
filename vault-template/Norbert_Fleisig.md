## Biography

Norbert Fleisig was born October 7, 1875 in Krakow, Poland (GEDCOM, unverified). He operated a haberdasher store. His daughter [[Ruth_Fleisig]] married Samuel Salk, connecting the Fleisig family to the Salk hardware dynasty.

Norbert died August 25, 1924 in Providence, Rhode Island at age 48.
