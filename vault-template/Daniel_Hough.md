## Biography

Daniel Hough was born February 5, 1724 in Middletown, Bucks County, Pennsylvania. He was the son of **John Hough III** (1682-1744) and **Eleanor Sands** (1692-1744/1764). His father was born in Hough, Wilmslow Parish, Cheshire, England, and emigrated to Pennsylvania. The family was **Quaker**; Daniel's grandmother Hannah Thompson Rossell's occupation was recorded as "Quakers; emegrated to Pennsylvania in 1883" (sic, likely 1683 or 1703).

Daniel's father John Hough III was the son of John Hough II (b. ABT 1660, Cheshire, England; d. after 1710, Middletown, Bucks, PA) and Hannah Thompson Rossell (1667-1736), who emigrated from England to Pennsylvania. Eleanor Sands was the daughter of Stephen Sands (b. Feb 25, 1661, Thornley, Lancashire, England; d. Nov 19, 1732, Bensalem, Bucks, PA) and Jane Cowgill. The Sands family also emigrated from England.

John Hough III and Eleanor Sands had multiple children documented in Filae Family Trees: William, Mary (m. Preston), John IV, Jane, Stephen, Johann Wilhelm "William" Haugh, **Daniel**, Joseph, and Richard.

Daniel married **Mary Worley** (b. 1725; d. March 4, 1741). Mary died young at approximately age 16; the GEDCOM also indicates Daniel had a second family (FAMS @F500740@), though the second spouse is not named in the extracted records.

**The Hough/Huff surname change:** Daniel's original surname was "Hough." The surname transitioned to "Huff" in subsequent generations. By the time of Daniel's son James (b. 1768), the surname was recorded as "Huff," though MyHeritage records James's father as "Daniel Huff (born Hough/Hoagh), Sr." This surname evolution is consistent with phonetic spelling changes common among families migrating from Pennsylvania to the Virginia/Carolina frontier, where the silent "gh" in "Hough" (pronounced "Huf" or "Haw") was simplified to "Huff." The variant "Hoagh" also appears in the records.

Daniel's documented children (via the Huff surname) include:
- **James Huff** (b. Feb 14, 1768, Brunswick County, VA; d. May 31, 1842, Golden Grove, Greenville, SC; m. Rebecca Moseley Nov 9, 1793)
- **Lewis Huff** (b. ABT 1764, Brunswick, VA; d. Sept 22, 1855, Brunswick, VA)
- **Philemon Huff**
- **Reuben Huff**
- **Sarah Huff**

Through James Huff's son [[Lewis_P_Huff]] (1803-1873), granddaughter [[Mary_Elizabeth_Huff]] (1838-1922), and the Long/Cox intermarriages, this Hough/Huff line connects to the Bright, Kascher, and Salmanson families.

Daniel died in 1752 in Stokes, North Carolina, at approximately age 28.

**Note:** Mary Worley's death date of March 4, 1741 (age ~16) and Daniel's death in 1752 suggest that Daniel remarried after Mary's death. James Huff's birth in 1768 is 16 years after Daniel's recorded death date of 1752, creating a chronological impossibility. This likely indicates either (a) Daniel's death date of 1752 is incorrect, or (b) James Huff's father was a different Daniel Hough/Huff (perhaps Daniel's son). This is an unresolved discrepancy requiring further research.
