## Biography

Yosef Bonam (also recorded as Yosef Bunim Walles) was the father of [[Yekutiel_Zalman_Wallis]] (1794-1872), who married [[Rivka_Beila_Schneerson]], daughter of the Mitteler Rebbe (GEDCOM, Geni.com).

Yosef Bonam was a son-in-law of Rabbi Levi Yitzchak of Berditchev (1740-1809), the renowned Hasidic master known as the "Berdichever Rebbe" (Family Tree, Geni.com). He married [[Luba_Tirtsa_Derbaremdiker]] (also known as Perl Walles, born Derbaremdiker), who was a daughter of Levi Yitzchak of Berditchev and Perl Lea Peretz (GEDCOM, FamilySearch).

The FamilySearch entry records his name as "Yosef Bunim Walles" with birth circa 1770 in Ukraine and identifies his wife as "Perl Walles (born Derbaremdiker)" (GEDCOM). He appears to have lived in the Berdychiv area of Ukraine.

Through his son Yekutiel Zalman Wallis, Yosef Bonam is an ancestor of the Zalmanson/Salmanson family line.
