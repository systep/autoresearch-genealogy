## Biography

Henie Markel is identified as the mother of **[[Heschel_Herman_Markel|Hersch Markel]]** on the 21 August 1894 Sambor Jewish marriage register (AGAD Fond 424, File 103, Record 34). She is listed as "Henie MARKEL" of Waniowice — meaning her recorded surname at the time of her son's marriage was MARKEL (the married surname she shared with her husband, Mordko Ber Markiel).

**Maiden name question.** The register does not explicitly record Henie's maiden name. The fact that the register lists her surname as MARKEL — the same as her husband's — admits three interpretations:

1. **Endogamous marriage within the extended Markel kindred** — plausible in Galician Jewish village communities, where cousin marriages and within-surname marriages were common. In this case Henie would be a Markel by birth, perhaps a cousin of Mordko Ber.
2. **Maiden name omission** — the register may have defaulted to her married surname because the maiden name was not readily available or the register's convention was to record the wife under her husband's surname.
3. **Abbreviated convention** — some Galician registers abbreviated the wife-of-groom's-father entry to match the groom's surname when the maiden name was unrecorded.

Without additional records (Henie's own birth record, or a separate document naming her father), her maiden name cannot be resolved. **Priority follow-up:** search Sambor Jewish Births 1825-1861 for any Henie / Chana / Hinde born to a Markel family (self-endogamy hypothesis) OR to any other family (would reveal maiden name).

The Yichus narrative identifies Mattis Markel as Heshel's brother, which implies Mattis is also Henie's son. Not corroborated by primary source but plausible.

Confidence: **Strong** on the fact of motherhood and the Waniowice residence (Tier 1 marriage record). **Unknown** on maiden name, dates, and parents.
