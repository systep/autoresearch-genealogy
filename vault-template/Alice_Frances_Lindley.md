## Biography

Alice Frances Lindley was born in 1736 and was a member of the prominent Lindley Quaker family from Chester County, Pennsylvania. She married John Baldwin circa 1730 and resided in Chatham County, North Carolina. She died in 1781.

An 1811 receipt signed "A Lindley" confirms her existence as a documented individual (Tier 1). The Lindley family was a well established Quaker family in Chester County before members migrated southward to North Carolina.

Note: The marriage date of circa 1730 and birth year of 1736 present a chronological inconsistency. Either the marriage date is later than recorded, or her birth year is earlier. This requires further investigation.
