## Biography

Elizabeth "Betty" Gresham was the wife of John Parker of Belton, Anderson County, South Carolina, and mother of [[Mary_Nancy_Parker_King]] (1787-1853), wife of [[James_King_Sr]]. Her son John **Gresham** Parker preserves the maternal maiden name as a middle name -- a Southern naming convention also seen in the Long-Goldsmith line ([[William_Goldsmith_Long]] preserves grandmother Sarah S. Goldsmith's surname).
