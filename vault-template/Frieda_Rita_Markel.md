## Biography

Frieda (also called Rita and Freda) Markel was the third of five children of [[Morris_Markel]] and Helen Brandt Markel, born c. 1925 in Galicia / Poland. The family emigrated to the United States c. 1928. She is documented in the 1940 and 1950 US Censuses in the Morris Markel household in West Warwick, Rhode Island, and in her mother's 1976 obituary as "Freda." Per [[Morris_Markel]] §Key Census Observations: "Frieda IS Rita." Same person, different names.

She is identified in the family-archive group portrait (chat-shared 2026-04-25) by [[Markel_Family_Portrait_Identification_Key]] bottom-row #12 ("Frieda Markel"). She is **NOT** [[Frieda_Karp_Weidenfeld]], who is a Karp cousin of similar name (and who was murdered in the Holocaust).

**Important: name disambiguation.** The Markel-line family archive contains at least three different "Frieda" persons:
1. **This individual** — Frieda (Rita) Markel, daughter of Morris Markel, US-resident.
2. **Frieda Frei Sternback** — Leah Frei's sister, emigrated to Brooklyn (Yichus, Tier 2).
3. **[[Frieda_Karp_Weidenfeld]]** — Friedelle, daughter of Feiga Frei Karp, Renya's mother, murdered in Holocaust.

The identification key entry "Frieda Markel" (bottom row #12) refers to **this individual** (Morris's daughter), based on the surname "Markel" and the bottom-row placement among Morris's other daughters.

**Confidence: Moderate.** Multiple primary sources (1940 + 1950 Census) plus secondary corroboration.
