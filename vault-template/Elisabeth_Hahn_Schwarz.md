## Biography

Elisabeth Hahn is documented in the FamilySearch index of her 8 January 1826 marriage at the Evangelical (Lutheran) parish of Krotoschin Stadt, Posen, Prussia (present-day Krotoszyn, Wielkopolskie, Poland). At the time of marriage she was 38 years old (→ born c. 1787-1788) and married [[Johann_Christian_Schwarz_Posen]] (age 24, 14 years her junior). The age gap strongly suggests Elisabeth had been previously married, and the parenthesized "(Schwarz)" on the FamilySearch index is ambiguous (either her post-1826-marriage surname by the groom, OR a prior-marriage surname). Without the original parish register image this ambiguity cannot be resolved.

If Hahn is her maiden name and Schwarz is her second-marriage surname, she may have been previously a Hahn, then a Schwarz (by 1826), then by another unrecorded Schwarz (her 1826 husband). If Schwarz is a prior-marriage surname, her maiden name is unknown. The most common FamilySearch convention parenthesizes the post-marriage surname, which would mean Hahn is maiden and Schwarz is her 1826 married surname.
