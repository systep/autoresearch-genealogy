## Biography

Rebecca M Long was born in 1898, the daughter of William Goldsmith Long (1862-1937) and Roxanne (Roxana) Cox (1865-1912). Through her mother, she descends from the Cox family of colonial South Carolina (Thomas Manning Cox → Thomas B. Cox → William Cox Jr. → William Cox Sr., who migrated from NC to SC in the 1780s). Through her father, she descends from the Long family who migrated alongside the Coxes.

She married **Albert Graham Bright** (1893-1961). By 1950, the family was living at **Sagamore Drive, Irondequoit, Monroe County, New York** (a suburb of Rochester). Albert was 56, Rebecca 51, with sons George M (26), Samuel (20), and Robert (19) at home.

Their daughter **Mary Elizabeth Bright** (~1922, born Tennessee) married Henry Michael Kascher (1914, Youngstown OH). Their granddaughter Kathryn Ann Kascher married Alan Carl Salmanson.

Rebecca is the key link between the colonial South Carolina families and the present-day family.
