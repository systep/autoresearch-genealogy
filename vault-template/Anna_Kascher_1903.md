## Biography

Anna Kascher was born 17 May 1903 in Waldhütten, Transylvania, the sixth child of **Michael Kascher Sr.** and **Anna Binder**. She was named after her mother Anna Binder.

Her mother died 18 October 1915 when Anna was 12 years old. Anna would have emigrated to the United States with her father and surviving siblings.

The 1935 obituary of her father lists two daughters among survivors: "Mrs. Martin Winter (New York City)" and "Leslie Wilson (New York City)." Anna may be one of these, having married and taken a new surname.
