## Biography

Richard Mosley was christened on February 17, 1793, in Kingsley, Staffordshire, England, and born before September 7, 1797, in the same location (GEDCOM @I502537@, Tier 3). He is part of the Mosley/Moseley line in the GEDCOM.

The GEDCOM places Richard as father of Jonathan Jonas James Moseley (via family @F500768@). However, Jonathan is recorded as born in 1683, over a century before Richard's christening in 1793. This is clearly a data error in the GEDCOM: Jonathan cannot be the son of Richard. Instead, Richard is likely a descendant in a later generation. The family link in the GEDCOM appears to be incorrectly assigned.

The Moseley family has deep roots in Staffordshire, England. The possible connection to William Moseley of Virginia (the Rotterdam merchant who arrived in 1649) is through Staffordshire origins, as William Moseley named his Virginia estate "Rolleston Hall" after his ancestral family seat in Staffordshire (WikiTree Moseley-506).

Richard Mosley is in the deeper Moseley line that also includes Richard Thomas Mosley (b. 1879, Derbyshire), who is Jonathan Mosley's son in the GEDCOM.
