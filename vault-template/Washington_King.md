## Biography

Washington King is known only from the death certificate of his son [[Judson_King]] (SC Death Certificate File No. 7013, 1921), where he is listed as Judson's father. No other records have been located.

Given that Judson was born in 1841 in Greenville, SC, Washington King was likely born in the early 1800s (c. 1800 to 1820) and lived in Greenville County, South Carolina. His wife's maiden name appears to be "Shambler" on the death certificate, though the handwriting is partially legible and alternative readings (Shamler, Shanler) are possible.

His relationship to [[Kerben_King]] (or "Corbin King"), the documented father of [[William_I_King]], is unknown. Both are Greenville County King families of the same era; they may be brothers, father and son, or otherwise related.
