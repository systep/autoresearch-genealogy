## Biography

Marguerite Julienne Simone (also recorded as Margareta) was a French Huguenot refugee born on 21 March 1708 in Pfeffelbach, which was then part of France in the Rhineland-Palatinate region. She was the daughter of Johann Jacob Simone (b. 16 April 1682, Kusel, Bavaria; christened 19 January 1691, Herren-Sulzbach; d. 15 April 1740, Germantown Township, Philadelphia, PA) and Maria Agnes Braun.

Johann Jacob Simone married on 29 January 1704 in Bombogen, Wittlich, Rhineland, Prussia, Germany. The family emigrated from Germany to Pennsylvania, arriving via Rotterdam on 2 October 1727.

Marguerite married Johann Michael Brecht II in April 1728 in Heidelberg, Lancaster, Pennsylvania. The Brecht/Bright family became established in colonial Pennsylvania. Together they had at least ten children, many of whom adopted the anglicized surname "Bright."

Her brother Johann Nickel Simone is also recorded in the family tree.

Marguerite died on 21 March 1778 in Schaefferstown, Lebanon County, Pennsylvania, and was buried at Heidelberg UCC Cemetery in Schaefferstown.
