## Biography

Sheyna (Sheine) Boruchovich was the wife of [[Dovber_Shneuri]], the Mitteler Rebbe (2nd Rebbe of Chabad-Lubavitch), and is known as the "2nd Rebbetzin of Chabad" (GEDCOM, Geni.com).

She married Dovber Shneuri in 1788, when both were approximately 15 years old (GEDCOM). They had numerous children, many of whom married into other prominent Chabad families. Her daughter [[Rivka_Beila_Schneerson]] married [[Yekutiel_Zalman_Wallis]], continuing the lineage to the Zalmanson/Salmanson family. Her daughter Chaya Mushka married Menachem Mendel Schneersohn, the Tzemach Tzedek (3rd Rebbe), making Sheyna the mother-in-law of the successor Rebbe.

She outlived her husband by nearly three decades, dying in 1855 in Lubavichi (GEDCOM). She was approximately 82 years old at death.

A biography by Sholom DovBer Avtzon about the Mitteler Rebbe lists pertinent details about Rebbetzin Sheyna (GEDCOM note).

**Confidence: strong.** Wikipedia confirms her marriage to the Mitteler Rebbe. Multiple independent sources (FamilySearch, Geni.com, Chabad historiography) corroborate her identity and role. The "2nd Rebbetzin of Chabad" designation is well established.
