## Biography

Edmund Cowgill was baptized 23 November 1634 at Thornton in Craven in the West Riding of Yorkshire, the son of Edmund Cowgill Sr. (1601/2-1642) and Jenet (Rideogh) Cowgill, who had married at Thornton in Craven 30 June 1631. He is the fourth generation of a Cowgill family continuously attested in Thornton in Craven parish records from 1567, descending from Bryan Cowgill through John Cowgill of Lothersdale and Edmund Sr.

Edmund married Ellin Stackhouse (b. abt. 1639), probably c. 1660-1665 under Settle Monthly Meeting care (specific register entry not yet located). He and Ellin became Friends (Quakers) and their children were raised in the Settle MM community which covered the Thornton in Craven / Newton in Bolland / Settle / Easington area.

Edmund died in England before June 1682. His widow Ellin emigrated to Pennsylvania on the ship Lamb, arriving with children John, Edmund, Jane, and Jennett in late 1682. Their eldest son Ralph had preceded them on the Friends Adventure in May 1682.
