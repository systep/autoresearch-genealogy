**Resolution**: George Washington King Sr. married twice. Elizabeth [Shambler?] (b. ~1825) was the first wife and mother of the older children including [[Judson_King]]. **Lucinda Chamblee** was the second wife and mother of the younger children including George W. King Jr. The 10-year gap between the last 1850 child (James M., b. ~1848) and George Jr. (b. 1858) fits a remarriage timeline. Elizabeth likely died between ~1848 and ~1857.

## Notes

This is a Tier 1 primary source: an official South Carolina death certificate. The informant, John W. King of Fork Shoals, SC, was likely a son of the deceased or a nephew ([[Judson_King]]'s son John W. King from the 1880 Census). The informant would have known the parents' names from family knowledge.

George Jr. resided at Pelzer, SC (RFD #3), the same town where H.H. King lived (informant on Judson King's 1921 death certificate). The King family had strong ties to both Pelzer and Fork Shoals.

The "Chamblee" surname is documented in the Greenville/Anderson County area. This opens a new family line for research.

George Jr.'s birthplace is listed as Greenville County (not Anderson County), confirming the King family moved from Anderson to Greenville between 1850 and 1858.
