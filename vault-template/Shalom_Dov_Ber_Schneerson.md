## Biography

Rabbi Shalom Dov Ber Schneerson, known as the **Rashab**, was the fifth Rebbe of the Chabad-Lubavitch dynasty (GEDCOM, unverified). He founded the Tomchei Temimim yeshiva in 1897.

He was the father of [[Yosef_Yitzchak_Schneerson]] (the Rayatz, 6th Lubavitcher Rebbe, 1880-1950) (unverified).
