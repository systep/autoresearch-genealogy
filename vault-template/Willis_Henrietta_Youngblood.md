## Biography

Willis Henrietta Youngblood was born November 23, 1863 in Columbus, Lowndes County, Mississippi. She was the daughter of **William Henry Youngblood** (b. May 1828, Bibb, Alabama; d. March 24, 1916, Chattanooga, Tennessee) and **Esther Lucy Kyle** (b. July 1827, Alabama; d. May 20, 1912, Chattanooga, Tennessee). William H. Youngblood was a landowner in Lowndes County, Mississippi.

The **1880 Census** records the family in Beat 5, Lowndes, Mississippi: William Youngblood (age 50), wife Lucy (age 52), and daughters Viola (22), Eleanor (19), and Willie (17).

Willis married **George Michael Bright** (1852-1939) on **September 18, 1890** in Lowndes, Mississippi. George was the son of Samuel Graham Bright (1825-1909) and Mary Jane Bernard (1829-1900), from the Greenville County, South Carolina Bright family of German Palatinate (Brecht) origin.

The handwritten family chart notes Willis as the **"1st Woman Graduate of U.S. [?]"**, though this claim is unverified and the notation is ambiguous (it is unclear which institution is referenced, and the claim itself requires substantial corroboration).

Willis and George had four sons: **Bernard Kyle Bright** (b. June 17, 1892, Abingdon, VA; d. December 16, 1978), **Albert Graham Bright** (b. August 19, 1893, Abingdon, VA; d. September 5, 1961, Delray Beach, FL), **William Wert Bright**, and **Arthur Wade Bright**.

Willis's census residences document extensive mobility: Lowndes, Mississippi (1880, 1890); Abingdon, Virginia (1900); Roanoke, Virginia (1910); Johnson City, Tennessee (1920); Big Lick, Roanoke, Virginia (1930); Kingsport, Tennessee (1935); Rochester, New York (1940).

Willis died June 28, 1952 in Bristol, Virginia, at the approximate age of 88. Her occupation at the time of death was recorded as "college school teacher." She was buried at **High Street Cemetery**, Rocky Mount, Franklin, Virginia.

Through her son Albert Graham Bright's marriage to [[Rebecca_Moseley_Long]], Willis is the great grandmother of [[Mary_Elizabeth_Bright]] (m. [[Henry_Michael_Kascher]]) and great great grandmother of [[Kathryn_Ann_Kascher]] (m. [[Alan_Carl_Salmanson]]).
