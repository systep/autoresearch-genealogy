## Biography

Yechezkel (Ezekiel) Luria appears in the GEDCOM as a key link in the Luria family descent. He is the son of Azariah Ben Avraham I (described as "Luria II [maharal Ancestor]" in the GEDCOM, b. ABT 1100, d. ABT 1150, @I501260@), and the father of [[Laemel_Luria]].

Yechezkel is positioned at the transition point between the "Luria II" line (which the GEDCOM explicitly marks as "[maharal Ancestor]") and the later Luria family proper. His father Azariah's line traces back through R' Avraham Ben Yekhiel (@I501261@, b. ABT 1080, d. ABT 1130) to Abu Nasr Yehiel Azariah (@I501262@, b. ABT 1060, d. ABT 1110), who is described as a grandson of Hai Gaon and connected to the ibn Nagrela family.

No independent historical documentation has been identified for this individual.
