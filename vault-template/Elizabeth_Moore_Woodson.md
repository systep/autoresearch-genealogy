## Biography

Elizabeth Moore was born in 1742 in Goochland County, Virginia, daughter of John Moore (b. 1706, d. before 1777) and Mary Jouett. She married Tucker Woodson Jr. in 1765 in Albemarle County, Virginia. After Tucker Jr.'s death in 1779 during the Revolutionary War, she remarried a Mr. Crockett (per her WikiTree married name "Crockett"). She died in 1820. Her son Tucker Moore Woodson (1777-1831) is recorded in the vault under the name "Tucker Woodson" and is the father of [[Mary_Ann_Woodson_Cooley|Mary Ann Woodson Cooley]] (1803-1850).

The Jouett line (her mother's surname) is significant in Virginia colonial genealogy; Mary Jouett's family is the Jouett family of the noted Captain Jack Jouett of the Virginia Revolution. This Jouett connection is uncorroborated here and should not be treated as established without further verification.
