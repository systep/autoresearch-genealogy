## Biography

Andreas Reinherd was a Transylvanian Saxon. He died in Petresti, Alba, Romania. He was the father of [[Katherina_Reinerth]] (b. 1813, d. 14 Jun 1876). The surname appears as "Reinherd" in the GEDCOM (I501372), while his daughter's married name source calls the father "Andreas Reinert." These are variant spellings of the Reinerth surname.
