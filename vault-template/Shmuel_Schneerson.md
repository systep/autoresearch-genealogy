## Biography

Rabbi Shmuel Schneerson, known as the **Maharash**, was the fourth Rebbe of the Chabad-Lubavitch Hasidic dynasty (GEDCOM, unverified). He was the youngest son of the Tzemach Tzedek, [[Menachem_Mendel_Schneerson_Tzemach_Tzedek]] (unverified).

He was the father of [[Shalom_Dov_Ber_Schneerson]] (the Rashab, 5th Lubavitcher Rebbe, 1860-1920) (unverified).
