## Biography

Rabbi Yosef Yitzchak Schneerson, known as the **Rayatz** or the **Frierdiker Rebbe** (Previous Rebbe), was the sixth Rebbe of the Chabad-Lubavitch Hasidic dynasty (GEDCOM, unverified).

His daughter [[Chaya_Mushka_Schneerson]] (March 16, 1901; died February 10, 1988, Brooklyn) married Rabbi Menachem Mendel Schneerson (1902-1994), who became the 7th and last Lubavitcher Rebbe (unverified).
