## Biography

Anna Margaretha Christmann was born on 1 January 1637 in Schriesheim, Baden-Wuerttemberg, Germany. She was the daughter of Commander [[Paul_Christman]] (b. 1605, d. 1666) and Anna Maria Herring. On 24 August 1658, she married [[Balthasar_Hans_Brecht]] (b. 24 Aug 1636, d. 9 Sep 1703), who served as councilman and almoner in Schriesheim. Together they had at least 14 children, including [[Johann_Michael_Brecht]] (b. 12 Oct 1662), who married [[Anna_Katherine_Hoffman]] and whose descendants emigrated to colonial Pennsylvania. Anna Margaretha died on 9 September 1703 in Schriesheim.
