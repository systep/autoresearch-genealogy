## Biography

Thomas Manning Cox (listed as "Thomas Manley Cox" on WikiTree) was the seventh child of Thomas B. Cox (1792-1857) and Olive Mary "Polly" Henderson. He was born in 1828 in the Greenville/Simpsonville, South Carolina area.

He married **Sarah Elizabeth "Lizzie" Clark** (1836-1879). Their daughter **Roxanne (Rooksaner/Roxana) Cox** (1865-1912) married **William Goldsmith Long** (1862-1937), uniting the Cox and Long families who had migrated together from North Carolina to South Carolina in the 1780s.

Through this marriage, Thomas Manning Cox is the direct ancestor connecting the colonial Cox family to Kathryn Ann Kascher (via Roxanne Cox → Rebecca M Long → Mary Elizabeth Bright → Kathryn Kascher).
