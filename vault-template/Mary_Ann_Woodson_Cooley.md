## Biography

Mary Ann Woodson was born 8 June 1803 (Find a Grave Memorial #25824984), the daughter of **Tucker Woodson** (1762-1831, FaG #209821208 corroborates a Tucker Woodson with matching life dates) and **Ann Scott Woodson**, per WikiTree Woodson-784. Per WikiTree, the Woodson line traces back to Mayflower ancestry; this claim is Tier 3 and not independently verified in this session.

She married [[Hiram_Cooley]] (1796-1864) of Greenville County, South Carolina, as his second wife (his first wife was Susan Cothran, by whom he had 2 children). With Hiram, Mary Ann had 10 additional children per the Cooleys and Crafts Family History Project narrative.

Mary Ann died 17 November 1850, age 47, predeceasing her husband Hiram by ~14 years. She is buried at the **Hiram Cooley Plantation Cemetery**, Greenville County, SC (FaG cemetery #2256431) -- the same cemetery as her husband (FaG #25824932), her in-laws Jacob Cooley Jr. (FaG #204500337) and Nancy Gover Cooley (FaG #204500423). The 1850 census closing date (per the 2026-05-02 Timeline Gap Analysis) lists "Mary Ann Woodson Cooley death 17 Nov 1850 Greenville SC" as a confirmed dated event.
