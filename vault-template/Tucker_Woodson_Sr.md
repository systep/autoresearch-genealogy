## Biography

Tucker Woodson Sr. was born c. 1720 in Goochland County, Virginia. Geni records his death as 1795; WikiTree Woodson-132 records "before 1809." He married first Sarah Hughes (mother of Tucker Woodson Jr., 1744-1779) and second Mary Netherland on 14 October 1762.

Tucker Sr. is the great-grandfather of [[Mary_Ann_Woodson_Cooley|Mary Ann Woodson Cooley]] (1803-1850), via his son Tucker Woodson Jr. (m. Elizabeth Moore 1765) and grandson Tucker Moore Woodson (1777-1831).
