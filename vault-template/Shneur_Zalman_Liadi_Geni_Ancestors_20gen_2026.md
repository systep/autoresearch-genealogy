# Geni: Ancestors of Admor Shneur Zalman of Liadi Boruchovitch (20 Generations)

## Source

- Original PDF: `/Assets/ancestors.pdf`

- 100-page PDF listing the 20-generation direct ancestry of Rabbi Shneur Zalman of Liadi (the Alter Rebbe, founder of Chabad-Lubavitch), compiled from the Geni.com curated tree.
- Extraction method: `pdftotext -layout` (preserves tree hierarchy via indentation)
- Source tier: **Tier 2-3** (Geni's curated World Family Tree is a collaborative secondary source with citations, but specific claims vary in quality from Tier 2 (well-documented 17th-18th century Ashkenazi rabbinic lineages) to Tier 3 (speculative medieval descents to biblical figures such as King David, and pre-Maharal ancestry).

## Structural Notes

Geni exports list each generation by number (`1.` through `20.` in the ancestors file; `1.` through `5.` in the descendants file). Indentation preserves the parent/child relationship. The text includes numerous repeated sub-trees because Geni walks each ancestor's full sub-tree even when the ancestor already appears in another branch. Lines marked "Duplicate tree section to be isolated, no need to merge" are Geni's own duplicate markers. Hebrew-only entries (in RTL script) represent profiles for which Geni has no transliterated English name and are preserved verbatim.
