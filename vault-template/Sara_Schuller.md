## Biography

Sara Schuller was a Transylvanian Saxon woman christened on 27 January 1829 in Waldhutten (now Valchid), in the Kis-Kukullo Megye region of Hungary (present day Sibiu district, Romania). She was the daughter of Johannes Schuller and Sara Benning. Per the user's research notes, Sara was previously widowed from a Stephann Benning before marrying Michael Kascher (1834, Wurmloch to 1880, Waldhutten). This prior marriage to Stephann Benning is not yet confirmed in the GEDCOM and requires further verification (unverified).

Sara and Michael Kascher had at least two children: Regina Kascher (b. 1861) and Michael Kascher (b. 1864), who later emigrated to the United States. Sara's siblings included Johannes Schuller, Michael Schuller, and Catharina Schuller.

Sara died in 1876. Her husband Michael Kascher died in 1880 of Lungenentzuendung (pneumonia) in Waldhutten.
