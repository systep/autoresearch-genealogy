## Biography

Betzalel ben Chaim Loew was the father of the Maharal of Prague. According to GEDCOM notes and loebtree.com, he was born c. 1480. Unlike his younger brothers who pursued rabbinic studies in Poland, Betzalel stayed behind to help his father Chaim in Worms (loebtree.com). He served as Rabbi and Rav/Av Beis Din of Prague (GEDCOM occupation field).

He married the daughter of Rabbi Chaim Issenheimer; an alternate tradition names his father-in-law as Rabbi Yitzchak Klober of Worms (GEDCOM note, citing Zeev Eshkolot, "The Eskeles Genealogy," p. 35). They had four sons and three daughters, with the Maharal being the youngest son (loebtree.com; GEDCOM note).

His tombstone is in the Old Jewish Cemetery at Josefov, Prague (GEDCOM).
