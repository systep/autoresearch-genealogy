## Biography

Catharine Schwartz appears in the 11 July 1870 US Federal Census at Sharon Township, Johnson County, Iowa (dwelling 43, family 45, line 33), as the 49-year-old Württemberg-born wife of John (Johann) Schwartz (49, farmer). Her maiden name is not recorded in the currently available index, and her parents are not named. Her birth year of approximately 1821 is derived from the census age.

The fact that both spouses gave their age as 49 in the same census suggests either a coincidence or a household informant rounding both ages to a single figure. Her husband's Tier 1 baptismal record places his birth in 17 May 1822; Catharine's birth is not Tier 1 corroborated.

The 1870 census marks her father and mother as "of foreign birth" (columns 11-12), consistent with her own Württemberg origin and an immigrant-generation family.
