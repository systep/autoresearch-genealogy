## Biography

Benjamin Salk was born in the mid-1800s in the Russian Empire (precise year unknown). He married [[Esther_Rappaport]]. Together they had at least one documented son, [[Julius_Salk]], born circa 1871 in Kovno (Kaunas), and this is the only fact currently attested by a Tier 1 source (the 1910 New York marriage certificate of Julius Salk and Rebecca Marcus, which names Benjamin as the groom's father).

No death date, residence, or occupation is recorded. The Kovno birthplace of his son strongly suggests Benjamin also lived in Kovno Gubernia, but this is inference, not direct evidence.

### Disambiguation from Benjamin Salk (b. 1889)

**This Benjamin Salk is distinct from the Benjamin Salk b. 1889 in St. Petersburg** who appears in the MyHeritage GEDCOM as a son of [[Nussen_Elia_Salk]]. The 1889 Benjamin is a full generation younger and born in a different city. Do not merge.
