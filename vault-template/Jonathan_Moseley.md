## Biography

Jonathan Jonas James Moseley was born in 1683 in England and settled in Surry County, Virginia. The GEDCOM records his place of birth with the notation "ofSurryCountyVirginia," suggesting the family was established in that county (GEDCOM, Tier 3).

Jonathan died before July 18, 1749, in either Surry or Brunswick County, Virginia (the GEDCOM records the death location as "Surry, Virginia" or "Brunswick County, Virginia"). He married Hannah Moseley (b. c. 1710, Surry, VA; d. c. 1749, Brunswick, VA) (GEDCOM, Tier 3).

Their son William Moseley (b. before 1715, Surry, VA; d. before August 26, 1776, Brunswick, VA) married Frances Moseley (b. 1724, Brunswick, VA; d. from 1748). William's son William "red-root" Moseley (b. 1744, Brunswick, VA; d. before April 25, 1808) continues the line.

The Moseley family of Surry and Brunswick counties, Virginia, may be connected to the earlier William Moseley who arrived in Lower Norfolk, Virginia, from Rotterdam in 1649 (see [[William_Moseley_1649]]). WikiTree's "The very earliest Moseleys in America" page documents these early Virginia Moseley families, though the specific connection between the Lower Norfolk Moseleys and the Surry/Brunswick branch has not been definitively established in this research.
