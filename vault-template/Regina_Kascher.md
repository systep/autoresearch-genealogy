## Biography

Regina Kascher was born 3 August 1861 in Waldhütten (Romanian: Valchid), Transylvania. She was the daughter of **Michael Kascher I** (1834-1880) and **Stephania Benning** (1829-1876; GEDCOM incorrectly calls her "Sara Schuller"; see cross_reference_audit.md #19). Per the **1938 Waldhütten church certificate "VATER"** (Tier 1, signed by Pastor A. Bogeschdorfer), she was the older sibling of **[[Michael_Kascher_Sr_1864]]** (b. 30 March 1864).

Regina's mother Stephania died 22 May 1876 of uterine cancer when Regina was 14. Her father Michael died 28 June 1880 of pneumonia when she was 18.

Unlike her brother Michael, who emigrated to Youngstown, Ohio, Regina appears to have remained in Waldhütten her entire life. She died 4 March 1933 in Waldhütten at age 71, two years before her brother's death in Ohio.
