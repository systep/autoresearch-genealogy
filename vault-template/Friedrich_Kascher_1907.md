## Biography

Friedrich Kascher was born 10 December 1907 in Waldhütten, Transylvania, the eighth child of **Michael Kascher Sr.** and **Anna Binder**. He was the **second** Friedrich in the family, a replacement name given after the first Friedrich (b. 15 April 1897) died on 7 February 1903 at age 5. This naming practice (giving a later child the name of a deceased sibling) was common in Transylvanian Saxon communities.

His father's 1935 obituary in the Youngstown Vindicator lists "Fred Kascher" among the surviving sons.
