## Biography

James Huff was born on February 14, 1768 in Brunswick County, Colonial Virginia (GEDCOM). He was the son of Daniel Hough (Huff), whose family had migrated from colonial Pennsylvania southward through the Carolinas. The surname transitioned from "Hough" to "Huff" during the family's southward migration.

James married Rebecca Moseley on November 9, 1793 in Brunswick, Virginia (Geni). Together they had nine known children. The family relocated from Brunswick County, Virginia to the Golden Grove area of Greenville County, South Carolina, following a migration pattern common among Southside Virginia families in the late 18th and early 19th centuries.

James and Rebecca's son Lewis P. Huff (b. Jul 22, 1803, Brunswick, VA) is an ancestor of Mary Elizabeth Huff, who married William Thomas Long (Family_Tree.md). This connection links the Huff line to the Long/Cox/Bright families and ultimately to the present family.

James's siblings (per Geni) included Philemon Huff, Reuben Huff, Lewis Huff, and Sarah Huff.

James died on May 31, 1842 in Golden Grove, Greenville, South Carolina (GEDCOM).
