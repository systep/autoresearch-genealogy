## Biography

James Huff was born on February 14, 1768 in Brunswick County, Colonial Virginia (GEDCOM, WikiTree Huff-1775). He was the son of **[[Daniel_Huff_Brunswick|Daniel Huff]]** (c. 1710/1720-c. 1777) and **Mary Holloway** of Brunswick County, Virginia. Daniel Huff's 1773 will (probated 24 Nov 1777 in Brunswick County) names James as one of five sons (WikiTree Huff-1770). James was named executor of the will alongside his brother Daniel Jr.

**Note on parentage correction:** The pedigree chart and GEDCOM incorrectly identify James's father as Daniel Hough (b. 1693, Middletown, Bucks County, PA; WikiTree Hough-149). WikiTree research (Huff-1770, Huff-1775) demonstrates that James's actual father was Daniel Huff of Brunswick County, VA, who married Mary Holloway. The Bucks County Daniel Hough married Elizabeth Dudley and is a more distant ancestor (or possibly unrelated). See [[Daniel_Hough]] and [[Daniel_Huff_Brunswick]] for details.

James married **[[Rebecca_Moseley_Huff|Rebecca Moseley]]** on November 9, 1793 in Brunswick, Virginia (WikiTree Huff-1775, Geni). Rebecca was the daughter of [[Samuel_Moseley]] and [[Martha_Patty_Holloway]] of Brunswick County. The Huff and Moseley families were connected through the Holloway family: James's mother was Mary Holloway, and Rebecca's mother was Martha "Patty" Holloway. Together James and Rebecca had at least nine children: Sarah (Huff) West, Mary H. Huff (b. Nov 15, 1797), Nellie (Huff) Turner (b. c. 1799), Martha Patsy Huff, [[Lewis_P_Huff]] (b. Jul 22, 1803), Amy Huff, Philemon Huff (b. Jun 25, 1809), Rebecca Jane (Huff) Payne (b. Sep 26, 1813), and possibly Avery Huff.

The family lived in Brunswick County, Virginia until about 1817, when James joined his brother Philemon in Greenville County, South Carolina (WikiTree Huff-1775). By 1820, James was living near both Philemon Huff and Samuel Mosley in the Golden Grove area (WikiTree Huff-446).

James and Rebecca's son Lewis P. Huff (b. Jul 22, 1803, Brunswick, VA) married [[Mary_Brashier]] and their daughter [[Mary_Elizabeth_Huff]] married [[William_Thomas_Long]]. This connection links the Huff line to the Long/Cox/Bright families and ultimately to the present family.

James's siblings (per Daniel Huff's 1773 will) included Daniel Huff Jr., Philemon Huff (c. 1763-1843), Reuben Huff, and Lewis Huff.

James died on May 31, 1842 in Golden Grove, Greenville, South Carolina (GEDCOM, WikiTree Huff-1775).
