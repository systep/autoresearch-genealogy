## Biography

Mary Holloway was the wife of Daniel Huff of Brunswick County, Virginia. Daniel's 1773 will (probated 24 November 1777, Brunswick Co VA Will Book) names "wife Mary" along with sons Daniel, Philemon, Reuben, Lewis, and James, and a granddaughter Tabitha. After Daniel's death, Mary administered his estate; **William Holloway served as her security on the administration bond**. The shared surname plus the security role make William Holloway a likely close relative -- most plausibly her father or brother, but not confirmed.

The use of "Holloway" as a given name pattern in descendant lines (Martha "Patty" Holloway Moseley, etc.) is consistent with Mary's Holloway origin. The pedigree-chart claim that James Huff's father was "Daniel Hough of Bucks County PA" is corrected at [[Daniel_Hough]] in the vault: the Brunswick VA Daniel Huff is the actual father, not the Bucks Co PA Daniel Hough.
