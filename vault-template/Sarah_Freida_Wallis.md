Sarah Freida Wallis was a daughter of [[Yekutiel_Zalman_Wallis]] and [[Rivka_Beila_Schneerson]] (GEDCOM, unverified). She married [[Israel_Noah_Schneerson]], the Rebbe of Nizhyn and a son of the Tzemach Tzedek (unverified).
