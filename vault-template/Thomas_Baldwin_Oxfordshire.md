## Biography

Thomas Baldwin was born 01 Nov 1657 in Oxfordshire, England, likely at Hook Norton (see [[Baldwin_Chesham_Quaker_Research_2026_04_19]]). He was one of three Baldwin brothers who sailed to Pennsylvania in 1682 as servants of Joshua Hastings, a Quaker. His brother [[John_Baldwin_Oxfordshire|John Baldwin Sr.]] also settled in Chester County; the third brother was Francis Baldwin (b. Hook Norton 20 Apr 1667, d. 1702 New Castle Co. Delaware).

Thomas worked as a blacksmith in Chester County, Pennsylvania. He left a will in Chester County (Tier 1, probate records), which dates his death to 1731.
