## Biography

Thomas Baldwin was born in 1657 in Oxfordshire, England. He was one of three Baldwin brothers who sailed to Pennsylvania in 1682 as servants of Joshua Hastings, a Quaker. His brother [[John_Baldwin_Oxfordshire|John Baldwin Sr.]] also settled in Chester County.

Thomas worked as a blacksmith in Chester County, Pennsylvania. He left a will in Chester County (Tier 1, probate records), which dates his death to 1731.
