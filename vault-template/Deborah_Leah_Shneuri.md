## Biography

Deborah Leah Shneuri was a daughter of the Alter Rebbe and mother of the Tzemach Tzedek ([[Menachem_Mendel_Schneerson_Tzemach_Tzedek]]). She married Shalom Shachne Altschuler (born Schneerson, 1772-1827). She died in 1792 at approximately age 26, when her son was only about three years old (GEDCOM, unverified).
