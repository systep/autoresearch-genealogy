## Biography

Katherina Reinerth was born in 1813 in Petresti, Alba, Romania. She was the daughter of [[Andreas_Reinherd]]. She married the older Mathias Pfaff (b. 1798 in Villingen, Baden, Germany; d. 1848). Their son Mathias Pfaff (b. 1836, d. 14 Dec 1899) married [[Katherina_Dressler]]. Katherina died on 14 June 1876 in Petresti. Also referred to as "Catharina Pfaff (born Reinert)" or "Catharina Pfaff (born Dalrackher)" in some FamilySearch records.
