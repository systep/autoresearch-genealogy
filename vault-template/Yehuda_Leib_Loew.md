## Biography

Yehuda Leib Loew was the great-grandson of the [[Maharal_of_Prague]], being the son of [[Shmuel_Loew]] (grandson of the Maharal) and grandson of [[Bezalel_Charif_Loew]] (son of the Maharal). The GEDCOM confirms his parentage through family link F500358 (Shmuel's family) and his own family F500327.

He married Sara Loew Katz (~1633-~1680), who was from Poznan, Poland. His son [[Moshe_Loew_of_Posen]] (1663-1736) continued the rabbinical dynasty in Poznan and eventually connects to the Chabad line.

Yehuda Leib lived in Prague for most of his long life (~1615-1706, approximately 91 years).

**Note on dates:** The user instructions list ~1625-1705, while the GEDCOM records ~1615-1706. The Family Tree file lists 1615-1706 with wife Sara Loew Katz (1633-1680). The GEDCOM dates are used here as they are more specific.
