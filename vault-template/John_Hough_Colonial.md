## Biography

This John Hough is recorded in the GEDCOM as the earliest Hough ancestor in this family line. He was born in 1642 in West Riding, Yorkshire, England, and is recorded as dying in 1659, also in West Riding, Yorkshire (GEDCOM @I501491@, Tier 3). He married Ann Heald (b. 1638, Middlewich, Cheshire). Their son John Hough (b. ~1660) married Hannah Worthington and emigrated from Cheshire, England to Pennsylvania as a Quaker, settling in Middletown, Bucks County.

The death date of 1659 creates a problem: if accurate, John died at age 17, which is very young to have fathered a son born around 1660. This may indicate that 1659 is an error, or that the death year means "1659 or later" (i.e., last known record), or that the son was born shortly before or after the father's death.

WikiTree profile Hough-146 describes a John Hough born c. 1653 in Wilmslow, Cheshire, England, who died c. 1732 in Middletown, Bucks, Pennsylvania. This appears to be the son of the GEDCOM John, not the same person. WikiTree Hough-146 married Hannah Worthington on October 19, 1680, and emigrated to Pennsylvania between 1681 and 1683.

The Hough family originated from the hamlet of Hough in Wilmslow Parish, Macclesfield Hundred, Cheshire, England. GEDCOM records confirm multiple family members with birthplaces at "Hough, Parish Wilmslow, Cheshire, England" and "Hough, Wilmslow Parish, Macclesfield Hundred, Cheshire, England."
