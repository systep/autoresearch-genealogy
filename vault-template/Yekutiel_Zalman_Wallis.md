## Biography

R' Yekutiel Zalman Wallis (also spelled Volis or Volem) married **Rivka Beila**, a daughter of the **Mitteler Rebbe** (Rabbi Dovber Shneuri, 1773-1827, the 2nd Lubavitcher Rebbe) and granddaughter of **Shneur Zalman of Liadi** (the Alter Rebbe, 1745-1812, founder of Chabad-Lubavitch). The marriage took place circa 1807.

His father was **Yosef Bunim**, who was himself a son-in-law of **Rabbi Levi Yitzchak of Berditchev** (1740-1809), one of the most prominent Hasidic masters. This places Yekutiel Zalman at the nexus of two major Hasidic dynasties.

The surname "Wallis" is a transliteration of **Volis**, a Ukrainian Jewish surname (possibly from the Yiddish personal name "Vole," a pet form of "Volf" meaning wolf). The family was based in the Ukrainian/Belarusian region.

His known children include:
- **Rabbi Levi Yitzchok Zalmanson** (1812-1872), who married Dvorah Leah Schneursohn (daughter of the Tzemach Tzedek, 3rd Lubavitcher Rebbe) in 1826 and was appointed to head a yeshiva in Lubavitch
- **Srul Mendel Zalmanson** (1820-1854), who died in Barysaw, Belarus; ancestor of the Salmanson family of Providence, RI
- **Sarah Freida** (c.1820), who married Israel Noah Schneerson (the Rebbe of Nizhyn), connecting the family further into the Schneerson dynasty
