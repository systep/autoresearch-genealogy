## Biography

Martha Ballard was born 1735 (Geni Tier 3 attribution) and married William Holloway of Brunswick County, Virginia. Her husband's 16 October 1784 will names her as wife and bequeaths her the plantation for life or until remarriage. The 1 April 1789 estate sale account references "the death of Martha his wife," placing her death between November 1784 and April 1789. She had at least seven known children (six living at her husband's death plus deceased son Obediah), among them [[Martha_Patty_Holloway|Martha "Patty" Holloway Moseley]] (~1755-1811), wife of [[Samuel_Moseley]] and grandmother of [[Rebecca_Moseley_Huff]] (1772-1837/38) -- placing Martha Ballard at the apex of the Brunswick VA Moseley-Huff-Long maternal line.

The Geni page title "Martha Holloway (Ballard)" with the (1735 - 1784) date attribution is the only direct birth-year attribution surfaced in searches; it may reflect a Tier 3 user-tree estimate rather than primary documentation.
