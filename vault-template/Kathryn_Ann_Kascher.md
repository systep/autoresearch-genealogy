## Biography

Kathryn Ann Kascher was born January 1, 1946 in New York, the eldest daughter of **Henry Michael Kascher** (b. December 10, 1914, Youngstown, Ohio) and **Mary Elizabeth Bright** (b. June 5, 1921). Her father was of Transylvanian Saxon descent (his father Michael Kascher emigrated from Waldhütten, Transylvania, Austria-Hungary). Her mother descended from colonial South Carolina families (Cox, Long, Henderson, Bright, Huff lines).

Kathryn had two sisters:
- **Nancy Jean Kascher** (b. March 1948, Rochester, NY), who married James Philip Wright and had a son, Sean Michael (b. November 6, 1968)
- **Suzanne Bright Kascher** (b. March 1952)

The family lived at 49 Clay Avenue, Rochester (Irondequoit), Monroe County, New York (1950 Census).

Kathryn married four times:
1. **Paul Gaffney**
2. **David** (surname unclear from handwritten chart)
3. **Richard Panciera** (Arizona; law profession noted on chart)
4. **Alan Carl Salmanson**

With Alan Carl Salmanson, she had two children: **Rebecca Salmanson** and **Leonard Daniel Salmanson** (L.D.).

The family resided at **1180 Narragansett Blvd. #C1, Cranston, Rhode Island 02905** (per a family letter).

### Ancestry

Kathryn's maternal ancestry traces back to colonial South Carolina through her mother's line. The complete path: SC colonial families (Cox + Long migration from NC to SC, 1780s) through Rebecca Moseley Long (1898-1985), who married Albert Graham Bright (1893-1961), whose daughter Mary Elizabeth Bright married Henry Michael Kascher, producing Kathryn.

Kathryn's paternal ancestry traces to Transylvanian Saxon communities. Her grandfather Michael Kascher (1889-1975) emigrated from Waldhütten, Transylvania (Austria-Hungary).
