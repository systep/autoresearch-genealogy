## Biography

Mary Nancy Parker was born 8 March 1787 in Anderson, South Carolina, the daughter of John Parker (b. 1753 Ireland) and Elizabeth "Betty" Gresham. She married [[James_King_Sr]], son of the Irish-emigrant [[Robert_King_II]] and [[Tabitha_Dolby]] who had arrived from Larne in 1770 aboard the ship *Jupiter*. The marriage links two Anderson Co SC Irish-emigrant farming families.

Mary and James King appear in the 1850 U.S. Federal Census, Eastern Sub Division of Anderson County, SC, as Family 398. James reported $3,000 of real estate. Their son [[George_Washington_King]] (b. 22 Dec 1815) is the great-great-grandfather of Judson King (b. 1841). Mary died 8 June 1853 in Anderson County, predeceasing James by ~14 years.
