## Biography

Georg Kascher was born 28 May 1895 in Waldhütten, Transylvania, the third child of **Michael Kascher Sr.** and **Anna Binder**. He was named after his paternal great-grandfather **[[Georg_Kascher]]** (b. March 1792, Wurmloch).

Georg emigrated to the United States in 1913 aboard the **SS Armenia** from Hamburg to New York. His immigration record lists "Anna Kascher (Mother)" as his relative in the country of origin, confirming his mother was still alive in Waldhütten at that time (she died in 1915).

His father's 1935 obituary in the Youngstown Vindicator lists "George Kascher" among the surviving sons. He was the second surviving son after Michael Jr. (b. 1889), as the eldest Friedrich (b. 1897) had died in 1903.
