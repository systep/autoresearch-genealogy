## Biography

Christian Weber was a Transylvanian Saxon. Per the **MUTTER church certificate** (Tier 1, 4 April 1938, Pastor A. Bogeschdorfer), he was the **Organist in Kreisch** (person E in the certificate), indicating he served as a church musician in the Evangelical A.B. community of Kreisch (Cris). He married **Sara geb. Valentin Kanasch aus Naschen** (from the Medvischer Stuhl administrative district; person F in the certificate). He was the father of [[Anna_Weber]], who married [[Martin_Binder]].

The GEDCOM renders his wife's name as "Sara Valetnin Manisch," which contains transcription errors. The MUTTER certificate (Tier 1) gives the correct reading as "Sara geb. Valentin Kanasch aus Naschen, Medvischer Stuhl."
