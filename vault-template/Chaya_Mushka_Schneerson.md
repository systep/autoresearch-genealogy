## Biography

Rebbetzin Chaya Mushka Schneerson was the daughter of [[Yosef_Yitzchak_Schneerson]] (the Rayatz, 6th Lubavitcher Rebbe) (GEDCOM, unverified). She married Rabbi Menachem Mendel Schneerson (1902-1994), who later became the 7th and last Lubavitcher Rebbe. She was named after her great-grandmother [[Chaya_Mushka_Shneuri]] (1789-1861) (unverified).
