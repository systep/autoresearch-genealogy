## Biography

Joseph Henry Rodbell was born July 27, 1886 in Poland (GEDCOM, unverified). He immigrated to the United States as a young man, probably via the Port of Baltimore (by analogy with the known Baltimore-based Rodbell immigrant bakers Solomon and Abram Rodbell documented by the Jewish Museum of Maryland). By 1911 he had relocated to Atlanta, Georgia, where he founded Capitol Hide and Metal Company, a scrap-metal business (Apex Supply corporate history, Tier 2). The business was inherited by his sons: Leonard Bland Rodbell pivoted it to Apex Plumbing Specialties in 1947, and Clyde A. Rodbell joined as equal partner in 1949. Joseph Henry died March 20, 1964 in Atlanta (GEDCOM, unverified).

His wife was Fannie Turetsky Rodbell (Clyde Rodbell 2015 obituary, Tier 2). This Fannie is distinct from the Fannie Rodbell who was Solomon Rodbell's wife in Baltimore.

The vault's GEDCOM lists his parents as [[Phillip_Rodbell]] and [[Ida_Rodbell]] (both ~1865, Russia), but this is Tier 3 and has not been corroborated by primary or secondary sources. The Breman Museum oral history interview with his grandson [[Sidney_Rodbell]] (11 January 2022, Aviary platform, Tier 2) should be consulted for first-person family narrative confirming parents and origin town.

### Bridge to Baltimore Rodbells (hypothesis, Moderate Signal)

See [[Rodbell_Origin_Research_2026_04_19]]. The Rodbell surname is a rare Ashkenazi Jewish surname; the Jewish Museum of Maryland documents Solomon Rodbell (baker, arrived Baltimore 1894 from Poland with wife Fannie and daughter Dora) and his older brother Abram Rodbell (Baltimore baker, arrived earlier). Joseph Henry's 1886 Poland birth makes him the right generation to be either (a) a nephew of Solomon and Abram who followed them to the US and then migrated south to Atlanta, or (b) their younger brother. The Atlanta-Baltimore migration was common for early-20th-century southern Jewish merchants. A documentary bridge (1900 Census, SS-5, or naturalization) is required to confirm the exact relationship.
