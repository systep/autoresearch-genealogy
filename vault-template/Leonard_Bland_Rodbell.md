## Biography

Leonard Bland Rodbell was the second husband of [[Thelma_Markel]] (Thelma Markel Salmanson Rodbell). Thelma married Leonard in 1978 after the death of her first husband [[Leonard_Irving_Salmanson]] in 1975 (Find a Grave #231959788). The couple lived in Atlanta, Georgia.

Leonard was born May 17, 1916 in Atlanta, Georgia, according to the GEDCOM (unverified). He died November 7, 1988 (unverified).

After Leonard's death, Thelma established the Thelma S. Rodbell Charitable Foundation (Cranston, RI; tax exempt since 1999), which has distributed $2.3 million in 386 grants since 2014 (ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer).

His relationship to other Rodbell family members in the GEDCOM (Clyde A. Rodbell, Joseph Henry Rodbell, etc.) is not established in this file but warrants investigation.
