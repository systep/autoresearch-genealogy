## Biography

Rivkah Loew Hirtz was born circa 1721 in Liozna, Belarus (GEDCOM). Her maiden name was Loew, and her father was known as Avraham "Der Gertner" (the Gardener) (user-provided data).

She was the mother of the Alter Rebbe, [[Shneur_Zalman_of_Liadi]], founder of the Chabad Hasidic movement. The GEDCOM records her profile name as "Rebitzen Rivka Lowe" (GEDCOM Smart Match records).
