## Biography

Yehuda Leib halevi Segal was born in 1720 in Russia (GEDCOM). He was recorded as also known by the name Yehuda Lajb Leib (GEDCOM).

He was a wealthy resident of Vitebsk who became the father-in-law of the Alter Rebbe ([[Shneur_Zalman_of_Liadi]]) through his daughter [[Sterna_Segal]]. Despite this family connection, Yehuda Leib is noted as having opposed his son-in-law's Chassidism (user-provided data).

He married [[Beila_Levinsohn]] by 1745 in Russia (GEDCOM). Residences include Bedzin and Ukmerge (GEDCOM).

Yehuda Leib died in 1918 in Vitebsk, Belarus and was buried in Safed, Israel (GEDCOM). Note: the 1720 birth and 1918 death dates yield a lifespan of approximately 198 years, which is clearly erroneous. The death date of 1918 likely contains an error, possibly 1818 (see Data Discrepancies).
