## Biography

John Parker was born 1753 in Ireland and died 4 June 1845 in Belton, Anderson County, South Carolina, age 92. He married Elizabeth "Betty" Gresham. Their daughter [[Mary_Nancy_Parker_King]] (b. 8 March 1787 Anderson Co SC, d. 8 June 1853 Anderson Co SC) married [[James_King_Sr]] (b. ~1787-88, son of [[Robert_King_II]] and [[Tabitha_Dolby]]).

The Anderson County SC settlement context strongly suggests John Parker was part of the same Larne, County Antrim emigration wave that brought Robert King II's family to America in 1770: both families were Irish, both settled in Anderson County (Belton area), and the ages (King II ~1750, Parker 1753) are compatible. A direct documentary link between the Parker and King families' Irish origin has not been located in free web sources.
