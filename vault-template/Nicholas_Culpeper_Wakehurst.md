## Biography

Nicholas Culpeper was the brother of Sir John Culpeper of Bedgebury (c. 1424 to 1480). Both were sons of Walter Culpeper, Esquire, and Agnes Roper (WikiTree, Tier 3; Geni, Tier 3).

**The Abduction of the Wakehurst Heiresses:**

Richard Wakehurst the elder of Wakehurst in Ardingly had a son, also Richard, who predeceased him. The younger Richard had married Agnes Gainsford and left two daughters: Margaret and Elizabeth Wakehurst, co-heiresses to the Wakehurst estate. After the younger Richard's death, his widow Agnes married Sir John Culpeper of Bedgebury, and the elderly Richard Wakehurst placed his granddaughters in Sir John's care (culpepperconnections.com, Tier 3).

Nicholas and his brother **Richard Culpeper** "descended on Sir John's and Agnes Culpeper's home at Goudhurst, dressed and armed for war and carried off the girls, who, according to the usual version of the story, wept piteously and loudly lamented their fate" (culpepperconnections.com, Tier 3). The girls were taken first to Bobbing, Kent (the residence of Alexander Clifford, husband of their sister Margaret), then to the house of one John Gibson in London.

This "high-handed proceeding" produced much litigation, as Elizabeth Wakehurst, the grandmother of the heiresses, refused to surrender the title deeds. Eventually a peaceable settlement was obtained.

Nicholas married **Elizabeth Wakehurst**, the younger co-heiress. His brother Richard married the elder co-heiress Margaret. Richard died without issue, but Nicholas and Elizabeth had a prolific family: **ten sons and eight daughters**, as recorded on their magnificent brass memorial in Ardingly Church, the largest family group on any Sussex brass (culpepperconnections.com, Tier 3; Metropolitan Museum of Art, Tier 2). The brass inscription reads: Nicholas Culpeper Esquyer "decessed the XXIII day of Maye ye yer of o Lord MDX."

Nicholas became the ancestor of **the Culpepers of Wakehurst**. His great-great-grandson was the famous herbalist **Nicholas Culpeper** (1616 to 1654).

## Connection to This Family Tree

Nicholas Culpeper of Wakehurst is the brother of Sir John Culpeper of Bedgebury, who was the father of [[Walter_Colepeper_Calais]]. Nicholas is therefore the uncle of Walter, and his Wakehurst line is a cadet branch of the same Culpeper family.
