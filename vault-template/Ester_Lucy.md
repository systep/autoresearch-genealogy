## Biography

Esther Lucy Kyle was born in July 1827 in Alabama. She married William Henry Youngblood (b. May 1828 in Bibb, Alabama; d. 24 Mar 1916 in Chattanooga, Tennessee). In the 1880 Census, she appears as "Lucy Youngblood," age 52, residing in Beat 5, Lowndes, Mississippi, with her husband and three daughters: Viola, Eleanor, and Willie (later [[Willis_Henrietta_Youngblood]]). Her occupation was listed as "Keeping House."

Robert Kyle Youngblood's death certificate names his mother as "Lucy Kyle." Esther Lucy died on 20 May 1912 in Chattanooga, Hamilton, Tennessee.

Also referred to as: Lucy Youngblood, Ester Lucy, Lucy Kyle.
