## Biography

Beila Levinsohn (also recorded as Bila Lowensohn) was born circa 1727 in Russia (GEDCOM). Her father was Judah Leib Lowensohn (born Lowy) per Geni records.

She married [[Yehuda_Leib_Segal]] by 1745 in Russia (GEDCOM). Through her daughter [[Sterna_Segal]], Beila became the mother-in-law of the Alter Rebbe ([[Shneur_Zalman_of_Liadi]]).

Beila died in Vitebsk, Belarus (GEDCOM, Geni).
