## Biography

Rochel Tsine Deych was the mother of [[Barnett_Salmanson]] (Berko Zalmanson, 1883-1960), the immigrant patriarch of the Salmanson family in Providence, Rhode Island. She was the daughter of [[Zawel_Deykh]].

She married [[Levi_Itzhak_Zalmanson]] (b. 1851) on December 2, 1877 in Rokiskis, Rokiskis District, Panevezys County, Lithuania (GEDCOM, Family Tree). This marriage connected the Deych family to the Zalmanson line, which descends from [[Yekutiel_Zalman_Wallis]] and [[Rivka_Beila_Schneerson]] (daughter of the Mitteler Rebbe).

Rochel Tsine was Levi Itzhak's first wife; he later married Bella Measroch (GEDCOM). Together, Rochel Tsine and Levi Itzhak had at least seven children. Their son Barnett immigrated to the United States in 1904 on the SS Baltic.

The GEDCOM records her death date as estimated July 26, 1935, sourced from the US Obituary Index match for Barnett Salmanson, suggesting this date may come from Barnett's obituary mentioning his mother's death.

**Geographic note:** The marriage took place in Rokiskis (northeastern Lithuania, near the Latvian border). Barnett was born in Vilnius in 1883, suggesting the family moved between 1877 and 1883 (Family Tree).
