## Biography

Ann Heald was born in 1638 in Middlewich, Cheshire, England, the daughter of [[William_Heald]] (b. 1596, Bury, Lancashire) and Ellen Oldham (b. 1600, England). She married [[John_Hough_Colonial]] (b. 1642, West Riding, Yorkshire). Their son John Hough (b. ~1660) married Hannah Worthington and emigrated from Cheshire to Bucks County, Pennsylvania, as a Quaker (GEDCOM, Tier 3).

Ann died in 1659 in Copmanthorpe, Yorkshire West Riding, England. The GEDCOM also records an "Anne Heald Or Oldham" (@I501850@), suggesting some ambiguity about whether Ann used her father's surname (Heald) or her mother's (Oldham).
