## Biography

Rabbi Shmuel ben Bezalel Loew was the grandson of the [[Maharal_of_Prague]] through his son [[Bezalel_Charif_Loew]]. He served as a rabbi in Hessen, Germany (GEDCOM). He was described as head of the Prague community.

Born in Prague, he spent his career in rabbinical roles in both Hessen and Prague. His long life (1575-1655, approximately 80 years) spanned a tumultuous period in Central European Jewish history, including the Thirty Years' War.

His son [[Yehuda_Leib_Loew]] continued the lineage that eventually connects to the Chabad dynasty through [[Moshe_Loew_of_Posen]], [[Schneur_Zalman_Loew]], and [[Boruch_Poisner]].
