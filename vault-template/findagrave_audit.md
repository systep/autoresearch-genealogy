## Notes

- **Lincoln Park Cemetery, Warwick, RI** has incomplete Find a Grave coverage. 8 of 9 Salmanson family members confirmed buried there, but only 4 have memorials. Memorial creation opportunities exist for Samuel, Charles, Donald, Martha, and Hilda.
- **Standing Springs Cemetery, Simpsonville, SC** has partial coverage. James Cox and Rebecca Henderson have memorials; Thomas B. Cox and Rev. Henry Henderson may need memorial creation.
- **Cox Family Cemetery** (Find a Grave Cemetery #2138325) exists in Greenville, SC on property belonging to Carolina Concrete Co. Isaac Cox may be buried there but no memorial exists.
- The **Argaleus Hercules Henderson I** memorial (#11725276) attributes burial to Oakwood Cemetery, Richmond, VA, which conflicts with all genealogical sources saying he died in Chatham County, NC. The cemetery attribution is likely erroneous.
