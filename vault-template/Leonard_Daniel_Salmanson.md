## Biography

Leonard Daniel Salmanson was born on January 27, 1982 in Providence, Rhode Island (US Public Records Index). He is the son of Alan Carl Salmanson and Kathryn Ann Kascher (GEDCOM).

As of August 10, 2005, his residence was recorded at 980 5th Ave, Apt 14B, New York, New York 10075 (US Public Records Index).

Leonard is named as an inventor on US Patent Application US-2019236082-A1, "Entity resolution computing system," filed November 21, 2018 and published August 1, 2019. Co-inventors were Ben Hizak and Zav Shotan, with the assignee listed as Cherre, Inc. (MyHeritage patent record 10834:92999944).
