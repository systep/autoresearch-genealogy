## Biography

Mary Elizabeth Bright was born June 5, 1921 in Johnson City, Washington County, Tennessee (MyHeritage pedigree chart), the daughter of **Albert Graham Bright** (Apr 19, 1893, Abingdon, VA; d. Sep 5, 1961, Delray Beach, FL) and **Rebecca Moseley Long** (Sep 14, 1898, SC; d. Oct 20, 1985, Atlanta, GA). Her father was the son of George Michael Bright (1852-1939) and Willis Henrietta Youngblood (1863-1952). Her mother was from Greenville, South Carolina, the daughter of William Goldsmith Long (1862-1937) and Roxana A. Cox (1865-1912), connecting the family to the colonial Cox and Long families of the South Carolina upstate.

Her parents married **September 2, 1920** in Johnson City, Tennessee (MyHeritage pedigree chart; note: earlier vault transcription of handwritten chart read "1928" but 1920 is strongly preferred since Mary Elizabeth was born June 5, 1921). Rebecca attended Winthrop College. Albert worked in the Rochester, New York area.

**1930 Census:** Mary Elizabeth (age 8) appears in Irondequoit, Monroe County, New York, a suburb of Rochester (MyHeritage).

She married **Henry Michael Kascher** (b. December 10, 1914, Youngstown, Ohio), a research chemist at Eastman Kodak. Henry was the son of Michael Kascher (1889-1975, born Waldhutten, Transylvania) and Rosina Falk (1896-1999).

**1950 Census:** Mary Elizabeth (age 28) and Henry (age 35) were living at **49 Clay Avenue, Rochester, Monroe County, New York**, with their daughter Kathryn (age 4). Her parents Albert G (age 56) and Rebecca L (age 51) were at Sagamore Drive, Irondequoit, with sons George, Samuel, and Robert (MyHeritage).

Mary Elizabeth had three siblings: George M Bright (b. ~1924), Samuel Bright (b. ~1930), and Robert Bright (b. ~1931).

Their three daughters were **Kathryn Ann Kascher** (b. Jan 1, 1946), **Nancy Jean Kascher** (b. March 1948, Rochester, NY), and **Suzanne Bright Kascher** (b. March 1952). Kathryn Ann later married Alan Carl Salmanson, connecting the South Carolina colonial families to the Providence, Rhode Island Salmanson family. Nancy Jean married James Philip Wright; their son Sean Michael was born November 6, 1968.
