## Biography

Johann (Johannes) Schuller was born on 3 September 1797 in Waldhutten (Waldhütten), Siebenburgen (Transylvania), Austria. He was the son of [[Leonhard_Schuller]]. He married [[Sara_Benning]] and had at least four children: Johannes, Michael, Sara, and Catharina. His daughter Sara married Michael Kascher. Johann died in 1858.
