## Biography

Johann Schuller was born in **September 1797** in Waldhütten, Siebenburgen (Transylvania), Austria. Per the **1938 Waldhütten church certificate "VATER"** (Tier 1 primary source), he was the son of **[[Leonhard_Schuller]]** (b. circa 1770, Waldhütten), for whom further data is missing from the church registers.

Johann married **[[Sara_Benning]]** (b. December 1800, Waldhütten) on **20 January 1820**. They had at least four children: Johannes, Michael, **Sara** (who married Michael Kascher on 4 January 1860), and Catharina.

Sara Benning died **17 October 1855** of tuberculosis (Tuberkulose). Johann died **10 March 1858** of liver disease (Leberleiden), surviving his wife by less than three years.
