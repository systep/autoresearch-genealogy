## Biography

Hilda Mittleman was born in 1916 in Providence, Rhode Island, the daughter of Max and Fannie Mittleman. She had three siblings: Harry Mittleman, Joseph Mittleman, and Sara (Mittleman) Goldberg.

She married **Samuel Salmanson** (1912-2004), son of Barnett and Elizabeth (Salk) Salmanson. Samuel was a co-founder and lifelong executive of the Adams Drug Company, which grew to over 500 stores.

Hilda and Samuel had two children: **James A. Salmanson** (b. March 10, 1945, Providence), who attended Syracuse University ('67) and earned an MBA from Babson College ('70), rising to vice chairman of Adams Drug; and **Tobey**, who married Richard Oresman.

Hilda's grandchildren included Matthew Oresman, Heather Wong, Lauren and Marc Regardie, Brian Salmanson, and Nikki DeMartinis. She had six great-grandchildren.

Hilda also lived in Chestnut Hill, Massachusetts. After Samuel's death in 2004, she continued to reside in West Palm Beach, Florida.

She died in January 2016 at the age of 99 in West Palm Beach, FL. She was buried at **Lincoln Park Cemetery**, Warwick, Rhode Island.
