## Biography

Nancy Gover was born 1763 (probably Pittsylvania County, Virginia) and married [[Jacob_Cooley_Jr]] on 5 April 1787 in Pittsylvania VA. The Cooleys migrated to Greenville District, South Carolina, where son Hiram (b. 1796) would establish Cooley's Bridge. Nancy died sometime before 1844 (per WikiTree). She is the great-great-grandmother of [[Charstee_King|Charstee Cooley King]] via Hiram → John Joseph Cooley → Charstee.
