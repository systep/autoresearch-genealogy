## Biography

Nancy Gover was born 1763 in Prince George's County, Maryland (per Find a Grave #204500423), daughter of John Gover (1732-1790) and Elizabeth Duvall (1738-1820). She married [[Jacob_Cooley_Jr]] on 5 April 1787 in Pittsylvania VA. The Cooleys migrated to Greenville District, South Carolina, where son Hiram (b. 1796) would establish Cooley's Bridge. Nancy died in 1842 (per Find a Grave; vault previously had "before 1844" placeholder from WikiTree). She is the great-great-grandmother of [[Charstee_King|Charstee Cooley King]] via Hiram → John Joseph Cooley → Charstee. Buried at the **Hiram Cooley Plantation Cemetery**, Greenville County, SC (FaG cemetery #2256431).
