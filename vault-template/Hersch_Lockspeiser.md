## Biography

Hersch Lockspeiser is identified solely from the back-side caption of his portrait in `Assets/Lockspeisers-Mom's Maternal Aunt's Family.pdf` (page 2). The portrait shows a bearded man, head and shoulders, dark hair, beard fuller than groomed, ~30-40 years old. The back caption in blue ink reads "Tante Chawa's / husband", with "Hersch Lockspeiser" written sideways down the left edge alongside a partial number that appears to begin "192_" (possibly indicating a date c. 1920s) or could be a catalog identifier.

He is the husband of [[Chawa_Frei_Lockspeiser]], whose family-relationship label "Tante Chawa" places her as the maternal aunt of [[Florence_Markel_Mermelstein]] and therefore as a sister of [[Leah_Frei]] (see [[Chawa_Frei_Lockspeiser]] for the chain of inference).

Hersch and Chawa had 6 children pictured together in matching dark sailor suits at a studio sitting in 1936 (back of photo: "Zum Andenken / Jahre 1936"). The German-language inscription is consistent with the family's location in Galicia (still bilingual German-Polish-Yiddish in the interwar period), most likely the Lemberg / Lwów region.

**Confidence: Low.** Single Tier 2 source (photo with caption). Surname "Lockspeiser" is not common; targeted searches in Sambor / Lwów region Jewish records, Sambor Yizkor Book, Yad Vashem, and JewishGen are likely to produce primary-source corroboration. Not yet attempted.
