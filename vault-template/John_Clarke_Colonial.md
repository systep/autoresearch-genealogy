## Biography

John Clarke was born in 1558 in Great Munden, Hertfordshire, England. He married Elizabeth Lovelock (b. July 21, 1549, Great Munden, Hertfordshire; d. 1577, Great Munden). Their daughter Ellen Clark (b. 1576, Giggleswick, Yorkshire) married John (Johannis) Stackhouse of Giggleswick, connecting the Clarke line to the Stackhouse and eventually Cowgill, Sands, and Hough families (GEDCOM, Tier 3).

John Clarke died in 1579 in Great Munden, Hertfordshire. His wife Elizabeth died in 1577, before John, when their daughter Ellen was only about one year old (GEDCOM, Tier 3).

A note of caution: The birth of Ellen in Giggleswick, Yorkshire (1576) while her parents lived in Great Munden, Hertfordshire, is geographically incongruous. This may indicate that Ellen was born elsewhere and later moved to Giggleswick, or there is a data error in the birthplace attribution.
