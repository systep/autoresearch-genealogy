## Biography

Anna Salk was born July 22, 1893 in Rhode Island (GEDCOM, unverified), the daughter of [[Abraham_Zolly_Salk]] and [[Tauba_Salk]]. She was a sister of [[Elizabeth_Salk]] (who married [[Barnett_Salmanson]]), [[Lena_Fain]], and Marry Salk (who married Lyons).

Anna married **[[Samuel_Zipperman]]** (born March 18, 1888, Warsaw, Poland; died March 21, 1977, Atlanta, Georgia). The family settled in Georgia. Their children included Louis Zipperman (~1915-2007), Bette Zipperman (1918-2017), Shirley Zipperman (1922-1987), and Arnold Zipperman (b. 1929) (all unverified).

Anna died January 26, 1958 in Fulton County, Georgia at approximately age 64.
