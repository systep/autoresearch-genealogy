## Biography

Tabitha Dolby was born 20 April 1756 in Ireland. She emigrated to America in 1770 from Larne, County Antrim aboard the ship *Jupiter* alongside her future father-in-law Robert King I and her future husband Robert King II. The ship landed in Charleston, South Carolina; the family settled near Belton, Anderson County, SC c. 1772. She married Robert King II and bore 23 children, of whom 21 survived to adulthood. She died 14 May 1815 in Belton, predeceasing Robert II by 11 years.

The search snippet for WikiTree Dolby-84 notes Tabitha "was related to the Pools" -- a probable reference to a related Pool/Poole family also among the Larne 1770 emigrants -- but does not name her parents. Her birth date of 1756 is six years later than the "c. 1750" estimate retained in [[Family_Tree]] for her, but per Guard Rail #3 (no modifications during expansion) the Family_Tree value is not changed in this session.
