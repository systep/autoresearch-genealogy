## Biography

Helen Brandt was born approximately 1889 in Poland, likely in the Galicia region (her husband Morris Markel's family came from Vanyavitch, Galicia, near Lemberg/Lviv). She was the daughter of [[Joseph_Brandt]] and [[Brina_Goldbaum]]. Brina Goldbaum's cause of death is recorded as the Holocaust in the GEDCOM.

Helen married **[[Morris_Markel]]** (Moishe Myer / Morris Markel), who was also born in Galicia and died in 1963. The Yichus narrative (Tier 2) confirms Helen as "Helen Brandt Markel," wife of Morris. Morris served as a learned man (described as Rabbi) at Ahavath Sholom Synagogue in Arctic (West Warwick), Rhode Island (Yichus narrative, Tier 2).

### Immigration

Based on the obituary of daughter Kate (Markel) Kaplan (Providence Journal, Jan 13, 2006), Kate was "born in Poland" and "moved to W. Warwick, RI at the age of 3." Kate was born December 6, 1925 (Find a Grave #232002112), placing the family's immigration to West Warwick at approximately 1928. This means older children Thelma (b. 1920) and possibly Anne and Frieda were also born in Poland. The family settled directly in West Warwick (Arctic), where Morris's sister [[Minnie_Markel_Sternbach]] and her husband Abraham Sternbach had been established since 1912 as founding members of the local Jewish community.

### Children

Together Helen and Morris had five documented children:
1. **Thelma (Tobi) Markel** (Sept 12, 1920, Poland, Jan 22, 2011), married (1) [[Leonard_Irving_Salmanson]] (1947), (2) Leonard Rodbell (1978). Buried Lincoln Park Cemetery, Warwick, RI.
2. **Anne Markel** (m. Schlusselberg)
3. **Frieda Markel** (dates unknown)
4. **Kate Markel** (Dec 6, 1925, Poland, Jan 11, 2006; m. Irving Kaplan). Born in Poland, moved to West Warwick at age 3. Graduate of University of Rhode Island. Biochemist at Sloan Kettering Hospital, New York. Later lived in Atlanta, GA. Buried Lincoln Park Cemetery, Warwick, RI. (Kate Kaplan obituary, Providence Journal, Jan 13, 2006; Find a Grave #232002112)
5. **Joseph Markel** (dates unknown)

Note: The GEDCOM previously listed this daughter as "Kay Kaplan." Her actual name was Kate, per Find a Grave and her obituary.

### Dates Clarification

The birth and death dates for Helen in the GEDCOM use the "ABT" and "EST" qualifiers with the date "27 JUL" appearing in both birth (~1889) and death (~1975), which is a system artifact or placeholder, not an actual date. The year estimates may be approximately correct, but the month and day should be disregarded.

The Yichus narrative (Tier 2, primary family document by Ruth Gold, Oct 9, 2011) confirms the marriage of Helen Brandt to Morris Markel but provides no additional biographical detail about Helen herself (birth date, parents, etc.). Her vital information remains Tier 3 only from MyHeritage/Geni; the Yichus confirmation of her married name elevates only that specific claim.

## Research Leads

1. **Rhode Island marriage records (1853-1925)**: Search for Morris Markel / Helen Brandt marriage in RI State Archives index. Marriage likely took place in Europe (before 1920, when Thelma was born) or in the US after ~1928 immigration.
2. **1930 US Census, West Warwick, RI**: Should show Morris, Helen, and children including Kate (age ~5) in West Warwick. Would confirm immigration year, Helen's birth year, and household composition.
3. **Ellis Island / ship passenger manifests (~1928)**: Search for Markel family arriving from Poland ~1927-1929. Would list ages, last residence, and contact person in the US (likely Abraham Sternbach in Arctic, RI).
4. **Lincoln Park Cemetery, Warwick, RI**: Confirm burial of Helen and Morris. Headstone may provide exact dates.
5. **Rhode Island naturalization records**: Morris Markel's naturalization petition would list place of birth, date of arrival, and family members.
