## Biography

Helen Brandt was born approximately 27 July 1889 in Poland. She was the daughter of Joseph Brandt (b. ~1815; d. ~1891) and Brina Goldbaum. Brina Goldbaum's cause of death is recorded as the Holocaust in the GEDCOM.

Helen married **[[Morris_Markel]]** (Moishe Myer / Morris Markel), who was also born in Poland and died in 1963. The Yichus narrative (Tier 2) confirms Helen as "Helen Brandt Markel," wife of Morris. Morris served as a Rabbi at Ahavath Sholom Synagogue in Arctic (West Warwick), Rhode Island (Yichus narrative, Tier 2). Together they had several children, including Thelma Markel, Frieda Markel, Kay Kaplan (born Markel), Joseph Markel, and at least two others whose names are marked private in the tree (one with married name Salmanson, another Schlusselberg).

Note: The birth and death dates for Helen in the GEDCOM use the "ABT" and "EST" qualifiers with the date "27 JUL" appearing in both, which may indicate estimated or placeholder dates rather than actual known dates. These should be treated with caution.

The Yichus narrative (Tier 2, primary family document by Ruth Gold, Oct 9, 2011) confirms the marriage of Helen Brandt to Morris Markel but provides no additional biographical detail about Helen herself (birth date, parents, etc.). Her vital information remains Tier 3 only from MyHeritage/Geni; the Yichus confirmation of her married name elevates only that specific claim.
