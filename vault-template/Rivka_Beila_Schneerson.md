## Biography

Rivka Beila was born in 1797, the daughter of **Rabbi Dovber Shneuri** (the Mitteler Rebbe, 2nd Lubavitcher Rebbe, 1773-1827) and his wife **Sheyna/Sheine** (~1773/1774-1855). Through her father, she was the granddaughter of **Rabbi Shneur Zalman of Liadi** (the Alter Rebbe, founder of Chabad-Lubavitch, 1745-1812).

Rivka Beila married **Yekutiel Zalman Wallis** (also transliterated Volis; 1794-1872) circa 1807. Yekutiel Zalman was himself the son of **Yosef Bunim** (1770), who was a son-in-law of Rabbi Levi Yitzchak of Berditchev, one of the great Hasidic masters. The surname "Wallis" is a transliteration of "Volis," a Ukrainian Jewish surname.

Their children included:
- **Rabbi Levi Yitzchok Zalmanson** (1812-1872): appointed by the Tzemach Tzedek (3rd Lubavitcher Rebbe) to head a yeshiva in Lubavitch. Married Dvorah Leah Schneursohn (eldest daughter of the Tzemach Tzedek) in 1826.
- **[[Srul_Mendel_Zalmanson]]** (1820-1854): died in Barysaw (Borisov), Belarus. Father of [[Levi_Itzhak_Zalmanson]] (1851), grandfather of [[Barnett_Salmanson]] (1883-1960).
- **Sarah Freida**: married Israel Noah Schneerson, the Rebbe of Nizhyn.

Srul Mendel and Levi Yitzchok were brothers, both sons of Rivka Beila and Yekutiel Zalman. The Zalmanson/Salmanson surname derives from "Zalman-son" (son of Zalman), referring to the family's descent from Shneur Zalman of Liadi.

The family's connection to the Schneerson dynasty runs through both maternal and fraternal lines: Rivka Beila was the Mitteler Rebbe's daughter (maternal connection), and her son Rabbi Levi Yitzchok married a daughter of the Tzemach Tzedek (fraternal marriage connection).
