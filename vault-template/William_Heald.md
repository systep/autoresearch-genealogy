## Biography

William Heald was born on January 18, 1596, in Saint Mary, Bury, Lancashire, England, the son of Richard Heald (b. 1574, Bury, Lancashire). He married Ellen Oldham (b. 1600, England; d. 1667, England). Their daughter Ann Heald (b. 1638, Middlewich, Cheshire) married John Hough, connecting the Heald family to the Hough/Huff line that would emigrate to colonial Pennsylvania (GEDCOM, Tier 3).

William died after 1638 in West Yorkshire, England. The "after 1638" date suggests that 1638 was the last documented event (likely his daughter's birth) rather than an actual death date.

The GEDCOM also records an "Anne Heald Or Oldham" (@I501850@), suggesting some confusion about whether Ann's mother was a Heald or an Oldham. This is a stub file; further research in Lancashire and Cheshire parish registers is needed.
