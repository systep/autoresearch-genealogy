## Biography

Anna Binder was born on 1 April 1868 in Kreisch (Cris), a Transylvanian Saxon community. She was the daughter of Martin Binder and Anna Weber. Anna married Michael Kascher Sr. circa 1900 in Waldhutten (Valchid), Siebenburgen, Austria (now Romania).

The couple had several children, including Michael Kascher Jr. (b. 1889, Waldhutten), Georg Kascher (~1895), and Martin Kascher (b. 1912, d. 1913 in infancy). Georg Kascher's 1913 immigration record to New York lists "Anna Kascher (Mother)" as his relative in the country of origin.

Anna died on 18 October 1915 in Waldhutten of tuberculosis. Her husband Michael Kascher Sr. later emigrated to the United States and settled in Youngstown, Ohio, where he died on 20 July 1935. His obituary confirmed that "Mr. Kascher's wife, the former Anna Binder, died in 1915."

The Siebenbuerger.de community database confirms her identity as a Transylvanian Saxon, elevating confidence above Tier 3 alone.
