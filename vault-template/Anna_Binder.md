## Biography

Anna Binder was born on 1 April 1868 in Kreisch (Cris), a Transylvanian Saxon community. Per the **1938 Waldhütten church certificate "MUTTER"** (Tier 1 primary source, signed by Pastor A. Bogeschdorfer, 4 April 1938), her ancestry is fully documented from the church registers (Matrikeln) of Kreisch.

She was the "eheliche Tochter" (legitimate daughter) of **(A) Martin Binder** (b. 3 May 1831, Organist) and **(B) Anna geb. Weber**. Martin Binder's parents were **(C) Johann Binder (eigentlich Krauss)** and **(D) Esthera geb. Theil** from Malmkrog (married 15 January 1817). Anna Weber's parents were **(E) Christian Weber** (Organist in Kreisch) and **(F) Sara geb. Valentin Kanasch** aus Naschen, Medvischer Stuhl (the "Mediasch Chair," an administrative unit). Johann Binder's father was **(G) Georg Krauss** (Alias Binder), baptized 14 February 1794, son of Johannes, mother unknown.

The note at the bottom of the certificate states: "Sonstige Daten und Angaben finden sich in den Matrikeln in Kreisch nicht" (No further data or information can be found in the Kreisch registers). All persons named were of Evangelical A.B. and German (Arischer) descent.

Anna married **Michael Kascher Sr.** (b. 30 March 1864, Waldhütten) on **29 January 1888**. Together they had **10 children** (per the "KINDER" certificate, 10 February 1938). Anna died 18 October 1915 in Waldhütten of tuberculosis.

**Note on "MUTTER" certificate structure**: The certificate identifies Anna Binder's parents as (A)+(B), with parents of (A) being (C)+(D), parents of (B) being (E)+(F), and parent of (C) being (G). This is a standard maternal ancestry extraction from church registers.
