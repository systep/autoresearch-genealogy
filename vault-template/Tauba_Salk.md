## Biography

Tauba, known by many name variants including Toby, Tobe, Teibe, Tobrey, Theresa, Fannie, and Pheabe, was born circa 1850 in Russia (GEDCOM). She married [[Abraham_Zolly_Salk]] (Abraham "Charles" Zolly Salk).

Immigration records show she arrived in the United States circa 1898 or 1901 (the GEDCOM records two different arrival dates) (GEDCOM). Census records from 1910 and 1920 place her as wife and head of house relation in Providence Ward 3, Providence, Rhode Island (GEDCOM).

Her daughter [[Elizabeth_Salk]] (born circa 1887, Russia) married into the Salmanson family.
