## Biography

Kalman (Kalonymos) Luria appears in the GEDCOM as a pivotal figure: he is the last individual in the Luria surname chain before the transition to the Loew family line. His son is recorded as Leibish Kalman Loew (@I501145@, b. ABT 1260, d. ABT 1310), who bears the Loew surname. Through the Loew line, the descent continues to the Maharal of Prague.

The name "Kalonymos" (Kalman being its Yiddish diminutive) is associated with the prominent Kalonymos family of scholars who were active in the Rhineland from the 9th century onward, which is consistent with the claimed Luria family origins in the Alsace/Lorraine region.

The descent chain from Kalman to the Maharal runs: Kalman Luria > Leibish Kalman Loew > Elazar Loew (b. ABT 1280) > and eventually to Bezalel ben Chaim Loew, the Maharal's father.
