# Hypothesis: Parentage of Lawrance Hough of Chorley (d. 1668 Wilmslow)

## Subject

[[Lawrance_Hough]], of Chorley township, Wilmslow parish, Cheshire. Buried Wilmslow 19 Nov 1668. Father of Anne (chr. 1646), John (chr. 1653, PA emigrant 1683), and Samuel (chr. 1659). Estimated birth before 1605.

## Research Question

Who were the parents of Lawrance Hough? Candidates carried forward from prior research: Roger Hough of Chorley (c. 1540-1587) and Henery Hough (abt. 1540-1618) of The Hough, Wilmslow (WikiTree Hough-2234).

## Candidates Evaluated

### Candidate A: Son of Henery Hough (Hough-2234, d. 1618 of The Hough)
- Evidence against: Henery's 1618 will names sons John, Henry, Edward, Thomas, not Lawrance. Residence "The Hough" (a separate township in Wilmslow parish), not Chorley. Tier 2 evidence (published will abstract).
- Verdict: Unlikely. Demoted.

### Candidate B: Descent via [[Roger_Hough]] of Chorley (c. 1540-1587) through an intervening son
- Evidence for: Roger Hough was residing in Chorley, matching Lawrance's later residence. Edmund Houghe son of Roger (chr. 1577, res. Chorley) or an unnamed son could plausibly be Lawrance's father. Geographic continuity at Chorley township is suggestive (Tier 3 inference).
- Evidence against: No primary-source baptism c. 1600-1610 yet located naming Lawrance's father.
- Verdict: Geographically plausible, genealogically unproven. Most promising line.

### Candidate C: Independent Chorley Hough line not related to Roger or Henery
- Not ruled out. Would require searching the full Wilmslow parish register 1595-1615 for any Hough christenings, residence Chorley, with a son Lawrance.

## Current Position

**No Tier 2+ source has been located that explicitly names Lawrance Hough's father.** The hypothesis remains open. The best current candidate is descent from Roger Hough of Chorley through an unidentified intervening son (likely chr. Wilmslow c. 1565-1585, father of Lawrance c. 1600-1610). The Henery Hough Hough-2234 candidate is demoted because his 1618 will does not name a son Lawrance.

Per the project's guard rail 1 ("Do not fabricate ancestors"), no change is being made to [[Lawrance_Hough]]'s ## Vital Information father field. The candidate note there already reflects the current uncertainty.
