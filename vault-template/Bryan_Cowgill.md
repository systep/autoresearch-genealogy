## Biography

Bryan Cowgill resided in Thornton in Craven, Yorkshire, England. He was the son of Richard Cowgill (born in the 1540s). Bryan married a woman named Ellen (surname unknown).

Bryan and Ellen's son, [[John_Cowgill_Lothersdale|John Cowgill "of Lothersdale"]], was baptized September 27, 1572 at Thornton in Craven. John would go on to father Edmund Cowgill Sr., continuing the Cowgill line that eventually emigrated to America.

Bryan died on August 7, 1599 at Thornton in Craven. He is the grandfather of Edmund Cowgill Sr. and an early ancestor in the Cowgill line connected to [[Jane_Cowgill]], [[Ralph_Cowgill]], and the Sands/Hough families in the vault.
