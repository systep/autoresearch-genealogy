## Biography

Mary Olive "Polly" Henderson was born 29 March 1795, one of fifteen children of [[Ezekiel_Henderson]] (b. 1763 Chatham NC, d. 2 Nov 1846 Greenville SC) and [[Elizabeth_Brasher_Henderson]] (b. ~1764, d. 1839 Greenville SC). Her parents had married 10/17 October 1782 in Chatham County, North Carolina, and migrated south to Greenville County, South Carolina.

Polly married [[Thomas_B_Cox]] (15 May 1792 - 12 June 1857), son of [[William_Cox_Jr]] and [[Connie_Baker]]. The couple had at least 12 children (per vault Family_Tree.md children list, which matches independent search-snippet enumeration of "Rebecca 1813, William 1815, Harvey 1817, Sarah 1819, Margaret 1823, Thomas Manley 1826, Isaac 1828, Henry 1831, James 1833, Mary 1835, Jefferson 1837"). The will of Ezekiel Henderson (Greenville Co SC) explicitly lists "T. B. Cox and Mary his Wife" among the legatees, providing a Tier 1 documentary link.

Polly died 21 August 1876, outliving Thomas by 19 years.

**Family network**: Polly's daughter Rebecca Adeline Cox (1813-1894) married Henry Langford Henderson (1812-1886) -- a grandson of Ezekiel via Brasher Henderson (1790-1847) -- making Rebecca Adeline a first cousin once removed of her own husband. (Henderson endogamy, common in this Greenville network.)
