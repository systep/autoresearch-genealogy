## Biography

Samuel Graham Bright was born on October 17, 1825 in Greenbrier, Virginia (GEDCOM, FamilySearch). He was the son of George Michael Bright and Mary "Polly" McNeil (FamilySearch). His father George Michael Bright Sr. was the son of [[David_Bright]], connecting the Bright line back to the Pennsylvania Brecht/Bright family.

Samuel married [[Mary_Jane_Bernard]] on April 17, 1851 in Franklin County, Virginia (GEDCOM). His occupation was listed as "saddler" (GEDCOM).

Census records place him in Franklin, Virginia in 1850 and 1860, then in Rocky Mount, Franklin, Virginia from 1870 through 1900 (GEDCOM). He also briefly appeared in Nicholas, Virginia in 1860 (GEDCOM).

Samuel and Mary Jane had nine known children, the eldest being George Michael Bright (b. February 17, 1852) (GEDCOM). His siblings included Thomas Jefferson Bright, Sarah B. Wright (born Bright), Dr. David B. Bright, Mary D. Bright, John Moore Bright, Margaret T. Board (born Bright), Michael R. Bright, Abraham R. Bright, Mary J. Bright, Mary Jane Bright, Francis Ann Bower (born Bright), and Elizabeth C. "Lizzie" Bright (FamilySearch).

Samuel died on July 27, 1909 in Rocky Mount, Franklin, Virginia and was buried at High Street Cemetery in Rocky Mount (GEDCOM).
