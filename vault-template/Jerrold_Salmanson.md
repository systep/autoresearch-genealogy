## Biography

Jerrold A. Salmanson was born January 30, 1942 in Rhode Island. He was the son of **Charles S. Salmanson** and **Hannah H. (Kushner) Salmanson**. Hannah was born June 26, 1914 in Providence, the daughter of Jacob Kushner and Dora Alpert (per her Social Security application).

On the **1950 Census**, the family resided at **284 Slater Avenue, Providence, Rhode Island**. The household consisted of: Charles S. Salmanson (head, age 35), Hanna H. Salmanson (wife, age 35), Jerrold Salmanson (son, age 8), and Deborah M. Salmanson (daughter, age 4). All family members were listed as white.

Jerrold attended **Hope High School** in Providence, Rhode Island (class of 1959, per yearbook records in GEDCOM associated data).

He served as **President of Salmanson Properties, Inc.** in Providence, Rhode Island (per US Public Records Index business associations).

Jerrold's sister **Deborah M. Salmanson** (b. October 19, 1945, Providence; full name Deborah Gail Salmanson per SS application) married and took the surname Roberts. She died March 21, 2002.

According to MyHeritage family tree data (Tier 3, unverified), Jerrold married **Dale Azimow**, who died in 2020. Jerrold died in **January 2024**. These dates are unverified.

Jerrold's father Charles was a son of [[Barnett_Salmanson]] and [[Elizabeth_Salk]], placing Jerrold in the fourth generation of the American Salmanson family from Providence, Rhode Island.
