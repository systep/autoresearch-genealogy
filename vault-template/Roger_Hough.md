## Biography

Roger Hough was christened circa 1540 in Wilmslow, Cheshire, England. He resided in the Chorley township within Wilmslow Parish. On June 4, 1564, he married Margery Mottershed at Wilmslow. Their first recorded child, Henry Hough, was christened October 29, 1564, just months after the marriage. A second son, Edmund Hough, was christened March 21, 1577.

Roger was buried on May 5, 1587 at Wilmslow. He is an early ancestor in the Hough line that would eventually emigrate from Cheshire to Pennsylvania.
