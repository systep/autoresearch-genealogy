## Biography

Rehoboam was the son of Solomon and Naamah the Ammonite. According to the Hebrew Bible, upon Solomon's death, Rehoboam's harsh response to the northern tribes' request for lighter taxation led to the division of the United Monarchy. The ten northern tribes seceded to form the Kingdom of Israel under Jeroboam, while Rehoboam retained the southern Kingdom of Judah (including the tribe of Benjamin). He reigned approximately 17 years.
