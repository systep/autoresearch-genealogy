## Biography

Moshe Loew of Posen served as rabbi and Rav of Posna (Poznan), Poland (GEDCOM). He was a descendant of the [[Maharal_of_Prague]] through [[Yehuda_Leib_Loew]] and [[Shmuel_Loew]].

He married Sara Katz (1665-1736), daughter of Shneur Zalman Katz (b. 1600) and Rosa Luria (b. 1625) (Family Tree, GEDCOM). The Luria connection is notable: the Luria surname is one of the most distinguished in Jewish genealogy, connected to Rabbi Solomon Luria (Maharshal, 1510-1573).

Moshe died in Minsk at the age of 72-73, the same year as his wife (GEDCOM). His son [[Schneur_Zalman_Loew]] continued the line that leads to [[Boruch_Poisner]] and ultimately to the Alter Rebbe, [[Shneur_Zalman_of_Liadi]].
