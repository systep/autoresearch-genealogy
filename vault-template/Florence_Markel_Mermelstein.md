## Biography

Florence (Sura Feiga) Markel Mermelstein was born on **May 15, 1909** and died on **November 24, 1990** (Yichus typed family tree listing, Tier 2). She was the seventh of eight surviving children of **[[Heschel_Herman_Markel]]** (Heshel Markel; b. 1862; d. 1916) and **[[Leah_Frei]]** (b. 1864; d. 1940) (Yichus narrative, Tier 2). Her Hebrew/Yiddish name was Sura Feiga.

### Immigration

In **1923**, Florence arrived alone in the United States on a ship the family remembered as **"The Liberty"** (Yichus narrative, Tier 2). She was approximately 14 years old at the time of her immigration. A ticket had also been purchased for her sister Hannah, but Hannah refused to leave because she was engaged to be married (Yichus narrative, Tier 2).

**Ship name analysis (2026-04-18):** No transatlantic passenger vessel named "The Liberty" has been confirmed for 1923. The SS Liberty (1890) was a British railway vessel, not a transatlantic liner. The name is likely a family memory error. Florence's 1923 arrival falls within the Ellis Island database coverage (1892-1924) and her manifest should be searchable via Steve Morse One Step or the Ellis Island Foundation under her Yiddish name "Sura Feiga Markel" or a variant.

### Marriage and Family

Florence met her husband, **David Mermelstein** (Hebrew name: **Aaron Dovid**; 1909-1990), while working at **King Solomon's bakery** in New York (Yichus narrative, Tier 2). They married in **1936 in West Warwick, Rhode Island** (Yichus narrative, Tier 2).

Their children:
1. **Ruth** (b. 1937), the author of the Yichus narrative (Ruth Gold, nee Mermelstein)
2. **Hashy** (b. 1940)
3. **Lea** (b. 1945)

3. **[[Morris_Markel]]** (Moishe Myer), Rabbi at Ahavath Sholom Synagogue, settled Rhode Island
4. **[[Sarah_Markel_Tachner]]** (Tsirl)
5. **[[Avraham_Chaim_Markel]]** (murdered with family in Pogrom, 1939)
6. **[[Hannah_Markel]]** (murdered with family in Pogrom, 1939)
7. **Florence (Sura Feiga)** (this individual)

Florence's father Heshel died in 1916 when she was approximately 7 years old. Four of her siblings (Avraham Chaim, Masha, Hannah, and Pessel) were murdered along with their families during the 1939 Rosh Hashanah Pogrom in the Rodzinka Forest (Yichus narrative, Tier 2).

Confidence: Moderate. The Yichus narrative and typed family tree listing (Tier 2, primary family document by Ruth Gold, Florence's daughter) are the sources. As Florence was the author's own mother, this data is based on direct personal knowledge, lending it higher reliability within Tier 2. The exact birth and death dates (5/15/1909 and 11/24/1990) come from the typed family tree listing pages of the Yichus document, which provide more precision than the narrative section.
