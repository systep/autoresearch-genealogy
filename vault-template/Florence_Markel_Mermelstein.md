## Biography

Florence (Sura Feiga) Markel Mermelstein was born in 1909 and died in 1990 (Yichus narrative, Tier 2). She was the seventh of eight surviving children of **[[Heschel_Herman_Markel]]** (Heshel Markel; b. 1862; d. 1916) and **[[Leah_Frei]]** (b. 1864; d. 1940) (Yichus narrative, Tier 2). Her Hebrew/Yiddish name was Sura Feiga.

Florence met her husband, **David Mermelstein**, while working at **King Solomon's bakery** in New York (Yichus narrative, Tier 2). They married in **1936 in West Warwick, Rhode Island** (Yichus narrative, Tier 2).

Their children:
1. **Ruth** (b. 1937), the author of the Yichus narrative (Ruth Gold, nee Mermelstein)
2. **Hashy** (b. 1940)
3. **Lea** (b. 1945)

Florence's siblings, in birth order per the Yichus narrative:
1. **[[Minnie_Markel_Sternbach]]** (1887-1975), settled Rhode Island
2. **Avraham Chaim Markel** (murdered with family in Pogrom, 1939)
3. **Masha Markel Tachner** (murdered with family in Pogrom, 1939)
4. **[[Morris_Markel]]** (Moishe Myer), Rabbi at Ahavath Sholom Synagogue, settled Rhode Island
5. **Sarah Markel Tachner**
6. **Hannah Markel** (murdered with family in Pogrom, 1939)
7. **Florence (Sura Feiga)** (this individual)
8. **Pessel (Peppy) Frei** (murdered 1939/1940)

Florence's father Heshel died in 1916 when she was approximately 7 years old. Four of her siblings (Avraham Chaim, Masha, Hannah, and Pessel) were murdered along with their families during the 1939 Rosh Hashanah Pogrom in the Rodzinka Forest (Yichus narrative, Tier 2).

Confidence: Moderate. The Yichus narrative (Tier 2, primary family document by Ruth Gold, Florence's daughter) is the sole source. As Florence was the author's own mother, this data is based on direct personal knowledge, lending it higher reliability within Tier 2.
