## Biography

Rabbi Boruch Poisner (also known as Baruch HaTzadik, "Boruch the Righteous") was the father of the Alter Rebbe, [[Shneur_Zalman_of_Liadi]], founder of the Chabad-Lubavitch movement (GEDCOM, Geni.com). His birth surname was Loew, connecting him to the illustrious [[Maharal_of_Prague]] lineage through [[Schneur_Zalman_Loew]], [[Moshe_Loew_of_Posen]], [[Yehuda_Leib_Loew]], [[Shmuel_Loew]], and [[Bezalel_Charif_Loew]].

He served as a rabbi (GEDCOM). His aliases include "Father of The Alter Rebbe" and the Hebrew designation "Boruch The Tzadik" (GEDCOM). He was orphaned at approximately age 14 when both parents died around 1735.

He married Rivkah Loew (born Hirtz, b. ~1721), who is recorded as being from Liozna, Belarus (GEDCOM). Their most famous son, Shneur Zalman, was born September 15, 1745 in Liozna and went on to found the Chabad-Lubavitch Hasidic dynasty.

Boruch also had a sister, Devorah Leah Kerpil (born Loew, b. 1719) (GEDCOM).

He died in 1790 in the Vynohradiv region of Ukraine and was buried in Vinogradov.
