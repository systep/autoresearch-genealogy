## Biography

William Culpepper of Hunton and Wigsell was a Tudor-era English gentleman, Justice of the Peace for Kent. He was the son of [[Walter_Colepeper_Calais]] (Under-Marshal of Calais) and Anne Aucher (WikiTree Culpeper-39, Tier 3).

**"Most living Caucasian Culpeppers worldwide descend from him."** This statement from culpepperconnections.com (Tier 3) and Geni (Tier 3) indicates that William and Cicely Barrett are the common ancestors of the vast majority of Culpeppers/Culpepers globally.

William was of age (21) in 1530, placing his birth around 1509. A marriage settlement between William Culpeper, Esq., Justice of the Peace for Kent, and Cecily Barrett was made on January 4, 1530 (WikiTree Barrett-194, Tier 3; Geni, Tier 3).

**Cicely Barrett** (c. 1512 to before 1559) was the daughter of John Barrett of Aveley, Essex. Her ancestry has been traced to European royalty and to Charlemagne, Emperor of the West (Geni, Tier 3; this deep ancestry claim requires independent verification).

William and Cicely had **seven sons and two daughters** (WikiTree Culpeper-39, Tier 3):

1. **John Culpeper, Esq.** (eldest son)
2. **Francis Culpeper**
3. **[[Martin_Culpepper]]** (Dr. Martin Culpepper, 1540 to 1605; Warden of New College, Oxford; Vice-Chancellor of Oxford; father of Mary/Mercy who married Sir Samuel Sandys)
4. **Walter Culpeper**
5. **Thomas Culpeper**
6. **Richard Culpeper**
7. **Edmund Culpeper**
8. **Elizabeth Culpeper** (married John Wildgose)
9. **Anne Culpeper** (married Simon Edolphe)

William died on December 6, 1559, at about age 50, at Salehurst, Sussex, England (WikiTree Culpeper-39, Tier 3).

Confidence: Moderate. Multiple user-contributed sources agree on the basic facts. The 1530 marriage settlement is a primary legal document (Tier 1) cited by these sources but not directly consulted.

## Connection to This Family Tree

William is the father of [[Martin_Culpepper]] (Dr. Martin Culpepper of Oxford). Martin's daughter Mary/Mercy Culpepper married [[Samuel_Sandys_MP]]. The line continues: [[Richard_Sandys]] to [[Stephen_Sands]] to [[Eleanor_Sands]] to John Hough III and subsequent descendants.

William is also the ancestor of most living Culpeppers worldwide, making him a significant gateway ancestor.
