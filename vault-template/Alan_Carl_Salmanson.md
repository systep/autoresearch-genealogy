## Biography

Alan Carl Salmanson is the son of **Leonard Irving Salmanson** (1910-1975) and **Thelma (Markel) Salmanson** (1920-2011). His parents married in 1947.

Alan has two sisters: **Barbara Diana Salmanson** and **Carol S. Salmanson** (both confirmed on Thelma's Find a Grave memorial #231959788).

He attended **Moses Brown School** in Providence, Rhode Island, graduating with the **Class of 1969**. Moses Brown is a Quaker-affiliated independent school.

Alan worked in the family **Adams Drug** / **Brooks Drug** business. Samuel Salmanson's 2004 obituary noted that "his nephews, Jerrold and Alan" worked in the family enterprise. Adams Drug Company was founded in 1932 by Alan's grandfather Barnett and grew to over 500 stores in 14 states. The company was sold to Pantry Pride in 1984 for $100 million and the stores were converted to the Brooks Pharmacy trade name in 1986.

Alan married **Kathryn Ann Kascher** (b. January 1, 1946, New York). Kathryn had been previously married to Paul Gaffney, David (surname unclear), and Richard Panciera. Alan and Kathryn had two children: **Rebecca Salmanson** and **Leonard Daniel Salmanson** (L.D.).

The family resided at **1180 Narragansett Blvd. #C1, Cranston, Rhode Island 02905** (per a family letter).

Alan's cousin **Jerrold A. Salmanson** (b. January 30, 1942; son of Charles and Hannah Kushner Salmanson) also worked in the family business.
