## Biography

Srul Mendel Zalmanson was born in 1820. His father was Yekutiel Zalman Wallis, and his mother was Rivka Beila Wallis (born Shneuri). The "Shneuri" maiden name of his mother suggests descent from the Schneerson/Shneuri dynasty, descendants of Shneur Zalman of Liadi, founder of Chabad-Lubavitch Hasidism.

Srul Mendel had two wives: a first wife whose name is unknown, and Gita Zalmanson. His children include Berko, Liba, and Levi Itzhak (b. 1851), though the children list may be incomplete (Levi Itzhak is confirmed as his son via MyHeritage but does not appear in the truncated children list on his profile).

He died in 1854 at approximately age 34 in **Barysaw (Borisov)**, in the Minsk Region of Belarus. His son Levi Itzhak was only about 3 years old at the time of his death.

**Note on Schneerson connection:** The Chabad connection to this family appears to run through the MATERNAL line: Rivka Beila (Shneuri) → Srul Mendel → Levi Itzhak → Barnett. This is a different pathway than the previously hypothesized connection through Rabbi Levi Yitzchok Zalmanson's 1826 marriage to Dvorah Leah Schneursohn, though the two Zalmanson lines may be related.
