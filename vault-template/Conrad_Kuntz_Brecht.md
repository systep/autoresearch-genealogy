## Biography

Conrad Kuntz Brecht is the earliest confirmed ancestor in the Brecht/Bright line (user instruction). He was born circa 1563, with Neudorf in Baden as his primary location (GEDCOM), though the Filae genealogy database records his birthplace as Lindenfels in Hesse (Filae record 14100:1199549072).

Conrad married Catharina Claus Camerer on March 24, 1584 (user instruction; FamilySearch gives the marriage location as "Lindenfendel, Stk, Hessen, Germany" in one entry, and "Neudorf, Bruchsal, Baden, Deutschland" with date 1582 in another). Catharina was born circa 1569 in Neudorf and survived until 1664 (GEDCOM, FamilySearch).

Conrad and Catharina had multiple children, including Christoph Stoffel Brecht (b. 1591), who moved from Neudorf to Schriesheim and continued the line that eventually emigrated to Pennsylvania. Other children included Paul Brecht, Georg Brecht, Blasius Brett, Hans Brecht, Sebastian Brecht, and Anna Ottilia Brecht (FamilySearch).

Conrad died circa 1612 in Neudorf (GEDCOM).

His parents, Hans Braecht and Margretha, are listed in the Filae database but are not independently confirmed. The GEDCOM references a FAMC @F500506@, linking to a parent family.
