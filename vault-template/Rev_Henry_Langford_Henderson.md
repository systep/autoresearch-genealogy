## Biography

Rev. Henry Langford Henderson was born June 7, 1812, the son of **Brashier Henderson** (1790-1847) and **Elizabeth Taylor**. Brashier was a son of **Ezekiel Henderson** (1763-1846, Revolutionary War veteran, pension S6994) and **Elizabeth Brasher** (married October 10, 1782 in SC). Brashier's siblings included Argaleus Hercules Henderson II, Sarah, James, Nancy, John, Olive Mary "Polly," Frances, William, Rebecca Adeline, Thomas Lawson, and Delila. Brashier also had other children: Elizabeth Rebecca, Samuel P., and Frances E.

Henry married **Rebecca Adeline Cox** (1813-1894), daughter of Thomas B. Cox (1792-1857) and Olive Mary "Polly" Henderson (1795-1876). This was a **first cousin marriage**: Henry's father Brashier Henderson and Rebecca's mother Olive Mary "Polly" Henderson were **siblings**, both children of Ezekiel Henderson and Elizabeth Brasher.

Henry served as a minister. The family was rooted in the Standing Springs Baptist Church community of Simpsonville, Greenville County, South Carolina, which had been organized in 1818 in the home of James Cox.

Henry and Rebecca had **10 children**:
1. Jane Henderson
2. Mary Ann E. Henderson
3. Oliver Henderson
4. Frances Emeline "Emma" Henderson
5. Rebecca Caroline Henderson
6. Winfield Jasper Henderson
7. Annette Arline "Nettie" Henderson
8. William H. B. Henderson
9. **Thomas Langford Henderson** (b. Aug 11, 1855; d. Oct 12, 1930; married Mary Frances "Fannie" Cox, b. 1858, on Dec 25, 1877)
10. Julia H. L. Henderson

Henry died November 18, 1886 at age 74. He is buried at **Standing Springs Cemetery**, Simpsonville, South Carolina, alongside his wife Rebecca (d. 1894).
