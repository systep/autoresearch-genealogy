## Biography

John Mathews was born in 1659, the son of [[Governor_Samuel_Mathews|Governor Samuel Mathews Jr.]], Commonwealth Governor of Virginia (1656, 1660). After his father's death in 1660 and his grandfather Samuel Mathews Sr.'s earlier death, John was the heir to the family's extensive Virginia landholdings.

In 1678, John received a patent for 2,944 acres, described in the record as being granted to him "as grandson and heir of Samuel Mathews." This Tier 1 land patent confirms the lineage from Samuel Mathews Sr. through Governor Samuel Mathews Jr. to John.

John married Elizabeth Tavernor in 1684. Their daughter Elizabeth Mathews married David Holloway, connecting the Mathews line to the Holloway family in this vault.

A 1702 Virginia court record further confirms the family connections. John Mathews died before 1706.
