## Biography

Louis (Elizar)(Lazar) Salk was born circa 1840 in Lithuania (GEDCOM). He was the father of [[Abraham_Zolly_Salk]] and the grandfather of [[Elizabeth_Salk]].

The GEDCOM shows three family group entries (F500023, F500024, F500065), suggesting multiple marriages or family groupings. His first wife [[Rose_Salk]] was born and died in Lithuania. Louis died before 1910 in Providence, Rhode Island, at approximately age 69 to 70 (GEDCOM).

### Surname Analysis

The surname "Salk" is an Americanized form of **Zalk**, a Jewish name from Lithuania (Ancestry.com, Geneanet). The name Zalk is most likely a pet form of the Yiddish personal name **Zalkind**, which is itself the German Yiddish kinui (nickname) for the Hebrew name **Shlomo (Solomon)**. The name "Elizar" or "Lazar" (Louis's alternate name) is the Yiddish form of the Hebrew name Eleazar.

A **Zalk family from Troskunai (Trashkun), Lithuania** is documented on JewishGen KehilaLinks, with the earliest known member being Leyzer Zalk. His son Zalkind Zalk married Freyda, and their son Samuel Zalk (b. ~1830) later emigrated to America. Some members of this family settled in **Duluth, Minnesota**. Louis Salk's Lithuanian origin and approximate birth year (~1840) place him in the same generation and region as the Troskunai Zalks, but **no direct connection has been established, and 2026-04-19 research now tilts AGAINST the link**: the full Troskunai line (Leyzer > Zalkind > Shmuel > Max Mortkhel Zalk b. 1857) emigrated to **Duluth, MN in 1905**, not Providence; the destination mismatch plus the absence of any Providence-Troskunai record is evidence against. See [[Open_Questions]] OQ #18 (PARTIALLY_RESOLVED toward REFUTED).
