## Biography

Rabbi Israel Noah Schneerson was a son of the Tzemach Tzedek and served as Admor and Rabbi of Nizhyn, Ukraine (GEDCOM, unverified).

He married [[Sarah_Freida_Wallis]] (~1820), a daughter of [[Yekutiel_Zalman_Wallis]] and [[Rivka_Beila_Schneerson]]. This connected two branches of the Schneerson dynasty (unverified).

His children included Moshe Schneerson (1869-1883), [[Nachoum_Schneerson]] (1882-1938), and Yaacov Schneerson (b. 1879) (GEDCOM, unverified).
