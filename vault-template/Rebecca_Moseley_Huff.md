## Biography

Rebecca Moseley was born on April 11, 1772 in Brunswick, Virginia (GEDCOM, Geni). She was the daughter of Samuel Moseley and Martha Patty Holloway. Rebecca had numerous siblings, including "Tama" Thama Hunt (born Moseley), Huldah Moseley, Samuel Moseley (son), Hulda Moseley, Abigail Moseley, Anne Elizabeth Moseley, Sally Moseley, Dann Moseley, Sarah Moseley, Nelly Moseley, and Mary "Polly" Moseley (Geni).

Rebecca married James Huff on November 9, 1793 in Brunswick, Virginia (Geni). They had nine known children together. The family migrated from Brunswick County, Virginia to Golden Grove in Greenville County, South Carolina.

Rebecca is the source of the "Moseley" middle name that appears in the Long family. Her granddaughter (through son Lewis P. Huff) was Mary Elizabeth Huff, who married William Thomas Long. Their daughter Rebecca Moseley Long (1898, 1985) carried the Moseley name forward, eventually marrying Albert Graham Bright (Family_Tree.md).

Rebecca died on January 7, 1837 (or 1838, per variant sources) in Golden Grove, Greenville County, South Carolina.
